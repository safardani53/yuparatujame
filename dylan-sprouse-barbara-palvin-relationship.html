Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin relationship explained

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 11, 2024

FORMER Disney Channel star Dylan Sprouse is officially married to his long-time love, Barbara Palvin.

One month after announcing their engagement, the couple exchanged wedding vows, and now fans want to know more about their relationship history.

What is Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin's relationship timeline?

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin's love story began as most new-age relationships do, by sliding into someone's DMs.

The Suite Life of Zack & Cody alum slid into Palvin's inbox in August 2017 after meeting at a Harper's Bazaar party, but it was not until six months later that Palvin responded.

"I knew I wasn’t in a good mindset at the time, and maybe deep inside I knew it could be something more," Palvin explained during a joint interview with W Magazine, via Us Weekly.

During the same interview, Sprouse recalled a conversation he had with his manager while in China filming a movie and revealed that Palvin was still on his mind.

"I ended up booking a movie and going to China for a six-month shoot. My manager was with me and asked, ‘Dylan, is there anything you wish you had wrapped up before you left?’” Sprouse said.

“And for the first time in six months, Barbara popped in my head, and I thought, ‘I wish I had time to take this girl on a date’. Ten minutes later, Barbara texts me for the first time in six months.”

While their relationship might've started off slow, it was smooth sailing once she reached out.

Their relationship timeline, as reported by Us Weekly, includes:

What do we know about Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin's wedding?

Photos of Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin's marriage were released on Monday, July 17, 2023, by Pop Crave's Twitter page.

According to Us Weekly, the couple wed the weekend prior in Palvin's home country of Hungary. Dylan's twin brother, Cole, was spotted in the wedding photos.

One month ahead of his nuptials, in an interview with V Magazine, Sprouse revealed that the traditional first dance was his biggest concern going into the wedding day.

"I think the definition of marriage is very different for our generation than what it meant for the previous one," he explained.

"For me, at least, marriage is a promise of kind of loving you endlessly and being your partner. I’m nervous about the event.

"Frankly, it’s about the first dance. I’m nervous about a choreographed dance," he added.

"I’m not a good dancer. And I’m nervous about remembering the steps."

During the same interview, Palvin explained that to her, the engagement was more stressful.

"I’m really not nervous about getting married. I think the engagement stressed me more than the wedding," Palvin said.

"I mean, a man has time to prepare for the proposal—Dylan kept the ring for seven months before he asked the question—but for me, it was right then and there. I knew since we started dating that I want to marry him, but it’s a big decision."

Palvin also commented on her excitement over the wedding location.

"I’m excited to show that side of me and introduce my culture, the places I grew up, and the places I went to," Palvin said.

"I feel like a lot of people will learn even more about me this way. I know there was no way we would have not had a wedding in Hungary because my parents would have killed me."

Who is Barbara Palvin?

Outside of being known as Sprouse's wife, Palvin is known for her career as a model.

She was born on October 8, 1993, in Budapest and first rose to fame in 2016 after she appeared in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue.
