Meghan Markles sunglasses have a secret security feature and fans are seriously impressed by her

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, April 7, 2024

MEGHAN Markle has an enviable wardrobe - but she also has an impressive sunglasses collection too.

And it turns out that royal fans are discovering the secret security feature on her stylish Le Specs glasses.

Social media user @zerlinamaxwell uploaded a video wearing Meghan’s beloved Le Specs “Air Heart” glasses, which retail for £60.94.

The fan wrote: “When you are influenced by Meghan Markle to buy these Le Specs but realise there’s a surprise security feature!

“You can see who is walking behind you!

“Has anyone else noticed this about these Le Specs sunglasses?

“This is a helpful ‘feature’ that I randomly noticed. It’s not built in, it just happens.

“It’s just the reflection because of the shape.”

One of the times we’ve seen Meghan wear the Air Hearts design is during her glamorous New York baby shower in 2019.

She paired them with Hatch black skinny maternity jeans and a William Vintage Courrèges coat.

The Duchess of Sussex also wore the shades again in December 2021, during a shopping trip in Montecito, California, where she now lives with Prince Harry and their two kids, Archie, and Lilibet.

Numerous stars are also fans of the sunglasses, including Olivia Palermo.

The website states: “With oversized and dramatic proportions reminiscent of a cat eye, the style is designed in thick black and luxurious metal top bar inlay, then fitted with premium zero base khaki flat lenses.”

Fans were quick to comment on Meghan’s sunglasses, with one saying: “I just got my Le Specs and experienced that! 

“I was a bit caught of guard at first because I saw the reflection of people walking behind me.”

Another added: “The way I need them now!! I need that feature in all glasses.”

As an all-American fashionista, Meghan has always favoured a pair of designer sunnies.

In one of her first public appearances with Prince Harry in September 2017, Meghan was pictured wearing a pair of playful 1950s tortoiseshell frames by Finlay London.

Meghan's Invictus Games tortoiseshell sunnies are available from £150 from the Finlay London website.
