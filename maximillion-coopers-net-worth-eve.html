Who is Maximillion Cooper and what's his net worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, June 3, 2024

MAXIMILLION Cooper is a British entrepreneur who's married to rapper Eve.

The pair have been together since 2014.

Who is Maximillion Cooper?

Cooper, 49, is a businessman, racecar driver, and skateboarder.

He is best known as the founder of Gumball 3000, a group of companies that owns an apparel brand, a registered charity, and organizes an annual celebrity motor rally covering 3,000 miles across public roads, according to SKPOP.

What is Maximillion Cooper's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Cooper has an estimated net worth of $60 million.

The majority of his cash comes from his lucrative business ventures, which he founded in 1999.

The company is a blending of Cooper's main passions: cars, entertainment, fashion, and music.

He makes the majority of his money from the apparel business, which has the same name as the signature rally he runs.

The company collaborates with famous brands like Puma, Adidas, and Nixon.

Gumball 3000 has also released several cars, video games, and films.

Cooper began his career as a fashion designer.

The entrepreneur is also a serial philanthropist.

He established the Gumball 3000 charity organization, which helps underprivileged kids in the UK.

Every year, Cooper organizes gran auctions and donates the proceeds to charity.

Who is Maximillion Cooper's wife Rapper Eve?

Eve is a rapper, singer, actress, and television presenter from Philadelphia.

In 1999, she released her debut album Let There Be Eve...Ruff Ryders' First Lady, which reached number one on the Billboard 200, made her the third female rapper to accomplish such a feat, according to CheatSheet.
