Amazing Samsung Galaxy S9 deal costs 23 per month and is CHEAPER than retail price, saving you

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 27, 2024

NEED a new phone? This deal on the stunning Samsung Galaxy S9 will save you more than £400 on the handset.

The handset is only a few months old, so it's impressive to see such a huge saving so soon.

This particular deal gets you the Samsung Galaxy S9 for £23 per month.

The upfront fee is £110, but that drops to £100 if you enter the code MOBILES10 at checkout.

It's a Vodafone tariff that comes with 4GB of data each month, plus unlimited texts and call minutes.

The total cost over 24 months works out at £652, which is actually £47 less than the Galaxy S9's £699 retail price, too.

  • Samsung Galaxy S9 at Mobiles.co.uk for £23 per month – buy now

But the saving on this Galaxy S9 deal is even bigger than it seems.

If you bought the phone outright (off contract), you'd also need to buy a SIM plan.

Vodafone's closest SIM-only plan is a limited-time offer that gets you 5GB of data and 1,000 minutes for £16.50 per month.

That works out at £396 spread over 24 months.

Tack that onto the £699 cost of the phone and you get £1,095, which is far higher than the total cost of today's contract deal.

So the true saving with this deal is actually £443 – not bad for a brand new smartphone.

The Galaxy S9 launched in April this year, and is currently Samsung's top handset.

It's got a huge 5.8-inch Super AMOLED screen with an eye-wateringly sharp 1440p resolution – even better than your Full HD telly at home.

The mobile is powered by Samsung's seriously speedy Exynos 9810 processor, and comes packing a generous 64GB of storage.

The Galaxy S9's standout feature is its design, however: the phone has an Infinity Display screen that wraps around the edges of the phone.

Samsung's attractive blower runs on Google's Android 8.0 Oreo operating system, which is a rival version of Apple's iOS.

It's got all the features and apps you'd expect, and is easy enough to use.
