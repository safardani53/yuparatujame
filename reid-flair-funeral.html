Funeral &amp; Private Burial Ceremony Details Revealed Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Reid Flair’s Funeral & Burial Ceremony Revealed

HollywoodLife.com spoke with the Flair family spokesperson who released this statement:

“On behalf of Ric and his family, thank you for your love, concern, and support this week. Reid’s funeral will be held at 1:00 pm on Wednesday, April 3 at Forest Hill Church (7224 Park Road Charlotte, NC 28210). The funeral will be open to family and friends who wish to pay their respects. (No photos or videos will be allowed and ample security will be present.)”

The statement went on to say: “The burial following the ceremony will be private for family members only. Please send cards, memorial donations, condolences, flowers, care packages, etc. to the Legacy Talent offices as we see Ric daily and will ensure he receives every delivery. All Memorial Donations will be given to PAL (Police Athletic League) in Reid’s name:

Ric Flair c/o Legacy Talent and Entertainment, LLC 1300 Baxter Street, Suite 100
Charlotte, NC 28204
(704) 334-7727

Thank you again for your overwhelming support. Despite the circumstances, we look forward to seeing friends and family Wednesday to celebrate Reid’s life.”

Reid’s 911 Call Released

Just moments after Ric found his son, he called 911. In the calls you can hear his father say: “He’s out cold! He’s out cold and he’s purple! Please! Please get a 911 team to my room! It’s a residential hotel on South Park. I’ve already called 911. Sir, what are you waiting for?!”

The investigation is ongoing but so far no foul play has been found.
