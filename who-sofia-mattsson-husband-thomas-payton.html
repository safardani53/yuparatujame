Who is Sofia Mattsson's husband Thomas Payton?

Sofia Mattsson has been married to Thomas Payton since May 14, 2016.

The pair reportedly dated for four years before tying the knot in California.

Payton is an actor, writer, and producer known for his work on the 2012 show, Campus Security.
