Shortly after settling into their new pad, the love birds realised that they wanted to swap the living and kitchen areas around.

The cooking space boasts light grey cupboards, white marble worktops and a huge bi-folding door that opens out onto their spacious garden.

Meanwhile, the former games room has now been transformed into a stunning living space with a dark navy feature all and matching U-shaped sofa.

They can also enjoy the stunning views from the comfort of their jacuzzi.

Their bedroom follows a neutral colour scheme and a bed that has a TV built into it.

And their downstairs toilets, master ensuite and guest ensuite in the property have all undergone amazing transformations.

Every room in the house is the perfect backdrop for one of their Instagram selfies.

They also have a utility room that features a shower for their pooches.

The grand staircase and hallway has been transformed from having traditional looking dark wood banisters and blue carpet into a chic grey and white haven.
