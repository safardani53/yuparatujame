How old is Trisha Goddard and is she married? The Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

TRISHA Goddard is a familiar face on our TV screens after hosting her own daytime talk show in the early noughties.

The presenter will be speaking to Piers Morgan on his Life Stories programme TONIGHT (February 25) but what else do we know about her?

Is Trisha Goddard married?

Trisha, 63, is not currently married but she is believed to be dating.

The former daytime TV presenter is keeping her new man's identity secret but has been living with him in Connecticut, US, since March last year.

She has said that her secret boyfriend is the first person she’s dated who has more money than her.

The talk show queen has been married three times before – first to Robert Nestdale.

Sadly Robert died of AIDS in 1989 and was secretly living as a gay man.

Trisha married her second husband Mark Greive in 1993 before their marriage ended just three years later.

She split from her third husband Peter Gianfrancesco in 2017 after 20 years of marriage.

On January 24, 2018 Trisha revealed on ITV's Lorraine that he made her feel "ugly and un-womanly" during her treatment for breast cancer.

Does Trisha Goddard have children?

Trisha has two children from her second marriage – Billie Greive, 31, and Madison Greive, 28.

Billie works in PR and Madison is training to be a mental health social worker.

Her third husband Peter became a father figure to his two step daughters.

Trisha recently opened up about her fears of passing HIV onto Billie when she was born after learning her first husband died of AIDS.

How did Trisha Goddard become famous?

Patricia Gloria Goddard (Trisha) was born in London in 1957 before moving to Tanzania for part of her childhood.

She made a move from travel writing to broadcasting before hosting her popular chat show Trisha, which first aired on ITV and then on Channel 5 between 1998 until 2009.

Her younger sister – who had schizophrenia – died from complications which arose from self-inflicted injuries leading to the TV host battling depression.

What TV shows has Trisha Goddard appeared in?

Trisha's talk show was one of the most popular daytime telly programmes in the Noughties.

It first aired on ITV from 2005 to 2009 but in 2012 moved across to Channel 5.

Unfortunately, it was cancelled after two years. Trisha then took her show to the US - but sadly, it was canned after two series.

Despite living in America, Trisha regularly makes appearances on British telly - recent shows include Jeremy Vince, Loose Women and Good Morning Britain.

The Sun revealed Trisha has been "desperate" to return to daytime television, with a source saying: “Trisha would love to bring her chat show back and is just looking for the right deal.

“She’s had a few meetings and is going to mull over her choices. She’s not in a rush to make any rash decisions and will make sure it’s the best option for her.”

Trisha also appeared on the 2020 series of Dancing on Ice alongside 11 other celebrities.

Did Trisha Goddard beat cancer?

The mother-of-two was first diagnosed with breast cancer in 2008 and she underwent chemotherapy while hosting her show.

Speaking about her battle with cancer, Trisha explained to the Sunday Mirror: “I never imagined I’d still be alive now, let alone make it to see 60. I can’t even think about what turning 60 actually means.

“I’m just b****y glad I’m still alive. And I can’t say the cancer won’t ever come back – I don’t know that, but I’ll do everything I can to help my health.”

Trisha gushed that her new clean living lifestyle has left her looking and feeling better than she has in years.

The star revealed she now runs at least five miles a day, practises resistance-based workouts while she has cut out meat and soya from her diet in order to keep herself in tip top form.
