Are Davey Havok and Brittany Bowen dating?

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Davey Havok is a well-renowned singer who prefers to keep his personal life away from the camera. This has certainly worked well for him as his love life is one of the biggest mysteries for his fans. 

While he has never publicly admitted to dating anybody, his pictures with a few women have been floating on the internet for quite some time now. It makes one wonder, who is Davey Havok dating?

Davey Havok Is Allegedly Dating Brittany Bowen

Famous Fix reported that he is currently in a romantic relationship with Brittany Bowen, a 37-year-old American model.

As per the outlet, they started dating in 2006 and have been going strong ever since. Even though he has never publicly acknowledged dating her, Havok has often been seen posing for pictures with his alleged girlfriend.

Not only that, in one of these pictures, the couple was seen kissing, which has fired up the rumor mill even more. Moreover, they are often seen hanging out, going to bakeries, and riding roller-coasters, among other activities. The rumored couple seems to be around each other frequently. 

Davey Havok With Rumored Girlfriend Brittany Bowen

Davey Havok Has Never Had A Spouse  

According to Who's Dated Who, before Bowen, Havok was engaged to actress and writer Monica Parker. They were building up to become man and wife for a year between 2001-2002. Apparently, things didn't work out well, and they decided to go separate ways. 

There were also rumors of him briefly dating Ivy Levan. However, this too was neither accepted nor denied by the singer. 

The celebrity has tried to keep his private life to himself, and while we don't know everything about his romantic relationships, we know that he has not taken a wife.

Yes, the singer is not married yet and seems to be extremely busy in his professional life. 

Davey Havok Is A Multifaceted Talent

David Paden Marchand, who works under the professional alias Davey Havok, is the lead vocalist of the rock band 'AFI,' the synthpop band 'Blaqk Audio,' the hardcore punk band 'XTRMST,' and the new wave band 'Dreamcar.' 

He knew from a young age that he wanted to make a career in the music industry. This is why he formed the band 'AFI' with his friends in high school.

Even though the band broke up after high school, he remained focused on music and began writing lyrics when he was in college. 

Years later, AFI reunited and started playing at shows for fans, and the rest is history.

Since then, the band has recorded ground-breaking albums such as Answer That and Stay Fashionable, Very Proud of Ya, among others.

He has also launched his short-lived but much-loved fashion lines. 

To add to that, Havok has played the role of an antagonist in the film series Godkiller and has been a part of Green Day's 'American Idiot.' 

The celebrated songster has also written two books titled 'Pop Kids' for children and 'Love Fast Los Angeles.' 

The vegan celebrity has also won the World's Sexiest Vegetarian contest by PETA in 2007, 2012, and 2017.
