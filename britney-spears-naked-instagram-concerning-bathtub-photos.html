Britney Spears sparks concern after singer posts totally naked bathtub photos with a 'bizarre' capti

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

BRITNEY Spears has gone totally naked in an unforgettably bizarre Instagram post on Sunday.

The megastar caused widespread concern and confusion with her caption about "liking to suck."

Britney, 40, covered her breasts and lifted her head to relax in the almost empty tub.

In the two candid photos, a small flower emoji hid her private area - where a pelvic tattoo was still visible.

The second snap was the same image re-shared with a slightly different Instagram filter.

"I like to suck !!" the pop star wildly began her caption with shrug emojis.

"Really?" wondered another. "I mean… same," wrote a third with a cry-laugh.

'I'M HOPING SHE WILL STOP'

Britney shares two sons with her ex Kevin Federline - Sean, 17, and Jayden, 16 - of whom she now has 30 percent custody.

Britney's youngest son Jayden shared his thoughts on her outpouring of naked Instagram snaps lately.

The teenager - who lives with his father Kevin - earlier told The U.S. Sun: "Social media helps her. So if that’s what she wants to do. I'm not going to hate her for that.

"At the same time, she should come to the realization of whatever it is that stops her from loving her family. It's almost like she has to post something on Instagram to get some attention."

"This has gone on for years and years and years and there's a high chance that this will never stop but I'm hoping for me that she will stop," he added.

Britney is now free from her conservatorship as of 2021, which for 13 years meant the pioneer of 2000s pop music couldn't post on social media, make creative or monetary choices without her family involved.
