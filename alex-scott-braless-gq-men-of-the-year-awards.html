Alex Scott stuns as she goes braless in see-through dress as she shows off new look at GQ Men of the

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

ALEX Scott looked incredible as she went braless in a see-through dress at the GQ Men of the Year awards tonight.

The 39-year-old dressed to impress as she posed on the red carpet of the event held at The Royal Opera House in London this evening.

Alex wowed in the silver glitzy floor length maxi dress for the annual ceremony.

The ex-England and Arsenal footballer completed the look with a pair of ankle boots which could be seen through her dress.

Alex rocked a smoky eye, nude lip and amazing contour.

The sports presenter also styled her hair into a slick back look.

Initially the pair wanted to keep their romance private, but shared dates before and after the Women's World Cup in the summer.
