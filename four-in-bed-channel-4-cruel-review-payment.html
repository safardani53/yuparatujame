Livid Four in a Bed hotel owner cuts off rival after being ripped to shreds with 'cruel' review

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, April 1, 2024

FOUR In A Bed stars were left riled after a series of "cruel" comments directed at their hosting skills.

In a special episode of the Channel 4 show, East Yorkshire-based Karl and Steph were branded too "over dramatic."

The pair own the Cedarville Guest House in Withernsea and felt snubbed by guests and fellow hotel owners, Sian and Mary.

In their feedback form, the Four In A Bed hosts had the chance to call out their visitors.

Steph told the pair: "To say that I was dramatic, it's nothing short of cruel, really cruel."

She previously added: "They obviously aren't in touch with humanity and other people's fears.“

Pressing the situation Steph quizzed her guests: "You said we were over dramatic at certain points, can you explain that please?”

Sian explained: “We just felt uncomfortable with the tarantula, we just felt you were being really over dramatic with…”

Yet Karl was quick to defend Steph and retorted: “Can I just stop you there, Steph has a morbid fear of spiders, that took some guts, let me tell you.”

Sian was referring to the group's visit to an animal experience where they got to hold a variety of creatures.

As a tarantula was brought out, Steph - an arachnophobic - hid in fear, prompting criticism for an alleged "over dramatic" attitude.

Meanwhile, the pair were just as annoyed after they felt they had been underpaid for the stay.

The popular Channel 4 show involves four B&B owners, who take turns to stay with one another – and pay what they consider fair for their stay.

The winner is the establishment named best value for money.

But fans were left fuming following a "tactical vote" from one set of B&B owners.

The show saw sparks fly as owners of the Wales-based The Griffin Inn saw Sian and Mary only fork out £30 out of the £60 cost for the room to Karl and Steph.

They also claimed they were "robbed of sea view room" despite The Cedarville Guest House not overlooking the ocean.

However, when the payment day arrived, and it was revealed Sian and Mary had underpaid because of such unfair reasons, Steph hit back saying: "I'm absolutely astonished.
