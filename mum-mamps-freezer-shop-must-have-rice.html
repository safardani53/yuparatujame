Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, April 22, 2024

ONE mum has revealed the £35 freezer food shop she did at M&S for quick and healthy dinner ideas during the week.

Kaye Marie, a mum of one, shared her food haul on TikTok, revealing the must-have item she won't buy from any other brand.

In the TikTok video, she said: "I've been in Marks and Spencer's and got five meals to put away in the freezer for quick and easy meals when we're on the go."

In the comments of the post, Kaye revealed it cost her £34.99 to be exact.

First the mum picked up her favourite pre-cooked rice form any supermarket, the M&S spicy rice.

She said: "I love fresh rice, but the only pre-cooked rice I will eat is Marks and Spencer's.

Kaye also picked up her staple BBQ chicken flatties which are perfect for tea when you're in a rush.

"Really quick with a flatbread when Louis on the go and he's got boxing or whatever."

Lastly, she picked up some mini tortillas wraps.
