Justin and Wife Hailey Spend Date Night at Hockey Match

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Justin Bieber and Hailey Bieber took their latest date night to the ice. 

On Wednesday, the couple attended the Toronto Maple Leafs' game against the Ottawa Senators at the Scotiabank Arena in Toronto.

Justin, 29, then shared a series of snaps from their outing on Instagram Thursday, including a sweet, smiling selfie of the duo in their seats and their view of the rink. 

“Had fun last night at the next gen game with the @mapleleafs,” the Canadian musician captioned the post. 

The pair dressed fittingly for the occasion, with Justin sporting a Maple Leafs jacket and Hailey a Maple Leafs cap. Hailey, 27, also showed her support with a blue roll-neck sweater in the team’s colors underneath her black leather jacket. 

Justin’s 292 million followers were quick to leave a flurry of gushing comments about the couple on the post, with one person seemingly a little disappointed they didn't get to tag along to the date night — Toronto native Drake. 

“Wow guy doesn’t even invite me to see some puck bunnies eh,” the rapper, 37, jokingly wrote in the comments section. 

Justin and Hailey’s outing at the hockey comes after they had another date night in Los Angeles earlier this month. 

The duo was pictured walking arm-in-arm as they headed to Italian restaurant Funke in Beverly Hills.

While Justin donned black oversized trousers and an equally oversized suit jacket with a white shirt and white snapback, Hailey stood out in a red jacket and a statement red lip. 

The Rhode Skin founder paired the outwear with sheer tights, black pointed pumps and black sunglasses.
