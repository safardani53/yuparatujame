The Naked Attraction couples that are still together - and one pair are even engaged

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 25, 2024

LOVE is definitely in the air as Naked Attraction proves its rather unusual dating style is helping individuals say goodbye to the single life.

The Channel 4 show which sees hopefuls bare all in an attempt to get a date is surprisingly a winning combination.

There are couples who are STILL together after meeting on the programme - and rather excitingly, one pair of lovebirds are even engaged.

Josh and Rob meet when they were in adjacent booths during series two.

Josh, a flight attendant from Crewe, was one of the first to leave the episode when he didn't get picked for a date, quickly followed by Rob, who was also unsuccessful.

But it was obviously meant to be as the two soon met up again after the show for a date - in New York.

And in 2018, the show shared the news of their engagement, tweeting: "we are so happy for this pair".

The show's host Anna Richardson told OK! that Rob and Josh were the show's "first ever engagement" and that she's "desperate for a naked wedding".

She explained: "They were in the pods but took a liking to each other off set.

"They met in the studio and hit it off and are together forever hopefully."

Naked Attraction first aired in 2017 and has had seven series so far.

It sees a contestant faced with six naked people who are slowly revealed from the feet up.

They then whittle the people down to two before stripping naked themselves and then deciding on the person they would like to date.

Another couple who are still going strong are Gemma Warren and James North.

They met on the first series when mum-of-two Gemma picked James from the line-up of unclothed men.

A year later they were already talking wedding bells and of their future together.
