RHONJ's Teresa Giudice shows off jaw-dropping Christmas decorations including all-white tree inside

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

TERESA Giudice has shown off the stunning Christmas trees and lavish decorations at her $3million New Jersey mansion.

The reality star is spending the holidays with her four daughters and her new husband Luis Ruelas.

Posting to her Instagram Stories on Christmas Eve, 50-year-old Teresa took fans inside her living room.

She showed off the over-the-top holiday decorations, beginning with her cozy fireplace.

It looks like Teresa is hosting multiple guests this year because 12 stockings are hung up, and each one is embroidered with a different name.

On the mantel sits a collection of coordinated silver Christmas ornaments, and she has placed two huge nutcracker statues either side of the fire.

Teresa is spending the holidays listening to vintage Christmas music, and Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas by Judy Garland could be heard in the background above the crackling fire.

The mom-of-four then panned the camera to reveal her stunning tree.

Teresa’s fir is decorated with sparkling fairy lights, white ornaments, and frosted pine cones.

The tree has been completed with a feathered topper, while fans could also see a huge pile of wrapped presents underneath.

ONE’S NOT ENOUGH!

But the self titled Queen of New Jersey doesn’t just have one tree in her home.

She also showed off a white artificial tree which stands in the hallway.

This one has been decorated with fairy lights but no baubles.

In the following slides, Teresa pictured some Christmas hampers which she has received from her Real Housewives of New Jersey co-stars.

She thanked Dolores Catania and her new boyfriend Paul Connell, and Jennifer and Bill Aydin for their gifts.

FAMILY FEUD

Fans might not be surprised to see that Teresa has not received a hamper from her brother Joe Gorga and his wife Melissa.

She has been locked in a feud with the couple in 2022 amid her decision to marry Luis.

Teresa refused to invite Melissa to the wedding, and was also accused of making allegations that her sister-in-law cheated on Joe.

The rift set tensions high between Teresa’s groom and her brother, who could be seen heading for a physical confrontation in a trailer for the new season of RHONJ, which premieres February 7.
