Maribel Aber Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 16, 2024

The CNN correspondent has mastered the art of slipping away from the paparazzi. She likes to keep her personal life intact to herself.  Information related to her love affairs and married life is still unknown but it is believed that she is single in recent days.

Aber attended the University of Virginia, majored in foreign affairs and the German language. She also joined New York Law School and received Juris Doctor. She began her professional career at the National Association of Securities Dealers, Inc.

From 2000, she began serving as an associate director at NASDAQ. Within a year, she was promoted to chief of staff for the vice-chairman of NASDAQ. She also hosted and produced the Wall Street Week and acted as the correspondent and host of The Broadway Channel. She has been working with CNN for five years, starting from 2012. 

She earns a good amount of salary from her Journalism career which is estimated to be around $ 500 thousand per year which adds up to her net worth that ables her to live a celebrity life.
