How Rich Is Emily Ratajkowski? Net Worth, Career, Salary

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Emily Ratajkowski and Sebastian Bear-McClard have split up. The former filed for divorce in Manhattan Supreme Court, and Page Six said that the case was disputed since the terms of the couple’s divorce had not yet been agreed upon. A source told People in July that Emily and Sebastian had divorced, claiming that it was Emily’s choice and that she was strong and focused on her kid. The revelation was first revealed by Page Six, and Emily was then seen on Instagram sans her wedding band.

Emily Ratajkowski’s net worth

Emily Ratajkowski is a well-known model, actor, and author who rose to prominence for her role in the Nickelodeon adolescent series iCarly. She then began her modeling career after being published in the sexy magazine goodies! in March 2012. CelebrityNetWorth estimates the 31-year-net-old’s worth to be about $8 million as of December 2023. She has amassed a substantial fortune as a result of her employment in the entertainment sector. After marrying Sebastian, the couple bought a home in Echo Park, Los Angeles. It is a $2 million residence with 1,650 square feet that was listed for $1.595 million. Emily paid more than the asking price for the home, which boasts a stone fireplace, skylights, and huge windows. Guests may stay in a separate cottage.

Emily made her feature film debut in 2014 with Gone Girl, a psychological thriller. Entourage, We Are Your Friends, I Feel Pretty, and Welcome Home followed. She has also featured in series such as The Spoils Before Dying and Easy. Ratajkowski has been in Sports Illustrated and walked the runways of New York Fashion Week, Paris Fashion Week, and Milan Fashion Week.

Emily Ratajkowski and Sebastian Bear McClard’s relationship

Emily Ratajkowski and Sebastian Bear-McClard were pictured together for the first time on Valentine’s Day, however, it’s unclear how they met. They dated for a while before getting married in New York City in February 2018. Emily shared how Sebastian proposed to her on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon. He proposed at the Minetta Tavern, she recalled, although he didn’t have a ring on him at the time. She continued, saying:

“And then he took the paper clip with which the money was paid and created me a ring, which I thought was quite lovely.”

The couple organized their wedding in such a manner that just a few people knew about it. Emily disguised herself in a mustard yellow outfit so that no one would see her. Everyone wore suits or sweatsuits, she said, while Sebastian donned a white jumpsuit.
