Stacy Keibler's Boyfriend Surprises Her With Trip, Bond-Themed Party

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

Now THAT's how you give a present! Stacy Keibler was gifted with one surprise after another by new boyfriend Jared Pobre this past week, topped off with an extravagant James Bond-themed birthday party on Monday, Oct. 14, that all but floored the Supermarket Superstar host.

"She knew he had planned the party but she had no idea that he was going to go all out," one partygoer tells Us Weekly of Pobre's sweet surprise. "She was blown away by how much effort he put into it."

The fete, which included mandatory costumes and models spray-painted gold, was held at the tech entrepreneur's home in Los Angeles.

In a cute shot Keibler posted on Instagram, Pobre, 38, dons a sparkly blue blazer and black bowtie, while Keibler, 34, shows off her killer legs in an elegant black gown. The pair lounge in high-back black arm chairs while flanked by models on either side of them.

"Thank u Mr. Bond for the best Birthday week of my life," Keibler gushed in the caption.

Prior to the costume party, Keibler and Pobre spent the week in Europe, stopping at Paris and Amsterdam along the way, where Keibler kept her fans updated with numerous stunning, scenic photos.

"It was incredibly romantic," a source close to the couple said.
