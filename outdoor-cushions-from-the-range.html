Shoppers rush to buy 'gorgeous' outdoor cushions from The Range for just 8 - just in time for summ

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 21, 2024

HAPPY shoppers are in a rush to clear these 'gorgeous' garden cushions off the shelves just in time for summer.

The Range shared a picture of the new exotic collection to its 1.2million Instagram followers - and fans were quick to reach for their purses.

Part of the outdoor range, these accessories come in vibrant emerald and deep blue colours - all for under a tenner.

The circular shaped cushions also come with different prints, ranging from palm leaves, trees, wild animals and psychedelic patterns.

The Mystique Outdoor Cushion, pictured above, is priced at £8.99 for the standard size, while the floor cushion will set you back £29.99.

For more simple decoration, there are some neutral comfy pillows on offer in beige and pale green such as the Ombre Raffia Outdoor Cushion - on sale for £19.99.

Bright tufted pieces with elegant buttons sewn onto the front are sold in yellow and blue - perfect for a glamorous garden party.

While they look aesthetically pleasing they could also double up as comfy garden seat covers.

In addition, The Range are also offering a vast selection of garden ornaments and decorations from plants to candle holders.

The brand captioned their Instagram post, which read: "Home styling doesn't stop at the front door anymore.

"Giving your garden some stylish accessories is a MUST for a truly serene space.

"AND Outdoor Cushions are an easy way to add some serious style and more colour to your outdoors!"

The picture was taken from inside their Erdington store in Birmingham, but online retail prices show the collection available from £7.99 to £9.99.

Shoppers went wild over the new range, one person commented: "Oh I love, I need a trip to The Range."

Other wrote: "I love these", "Gorgeous cushions", and "Garden inspo".

"That's a nice display. The Range near us is usual empty selves", said another, showing how quickly products are sold.

Items in the collection include the Boho Outdoor Cushion which costs £7.99, the Palm Outdoor Cushion at £8.99 and the Happy Place Raffia Outdoor Cushion priced at £9.99.

This comes as shoppers scrambled to get their hands on The Range's new hot water bottle.

The store took to Facebook to post a snap of their new Neck Hot Water Bottles, which are just £7.99.

Other fans also raced to buy the safari fairy lights sold for just £9.99 - perfect for a child's bedroom.
