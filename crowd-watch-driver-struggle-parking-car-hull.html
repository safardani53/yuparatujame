Stunned crowd gather to watch driver's baffling attempt to get into parking space

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 7, 2024

THIS is the toe-curling moment a female driver struggles to park her car in a busy town centre.

Footage shows by-passers looking on in astonishment as the driver woefully attempts to get her motor in between two others in Hull City Centre.

Initially the grey motor appears to be only whiskers away from the parked silver estate on the left as she jolts to and fro.

The woman can then be seen hoping out of her motor and asking the driver on her right to move before pulling out and driving away.

The painful footage was taken by onlooker Tony Roberts who was sitting in a nearby cafe.

He told the Hull Daily Mail: "I couldn't believe why she did it.

"I was shocked by her quality of driving. She drove to the dead end, turned around and then proceeded to try to park from the wrong angle."

As he filmed the struggle other onlookers can be heard in the background watch in disbelief.

Tony later decided to share the footage on social media which has been shared thousands of times.

Posting on Facebook he wrote: "Saw this in town today, she drove off after damaging the guys car, I left a note for him, he has the video evidence now."
