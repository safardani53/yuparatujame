Alexa Carole Havins is an American actress. She first rose to fame in 2003, when she became the lead actor as Babe Carey Chandler in the soap opera All My Children. Her flawed but benevolent role as Babe Carey earned her a Day Emmy nomination in 2005 and was recognized as one half of one of the show's most popular pair of soap operas. Havins has appeared in a number of television shows, as well as a number of independent films. In 2011, she joined the main cast of Torchwood's fourth series, Torchwood: Miracle Day, which aired on BBC Worldwide and the American TV channel Starz.
