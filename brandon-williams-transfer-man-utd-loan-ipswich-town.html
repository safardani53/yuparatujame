Brandon Williams leaves Man Utd on loan transfer as he links up with former club chief

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

MANCHESTER UNITED defender Brandon Williams will spend the season on loan with Ipswich Town.

The 22-year-old is set to reunite with former United youth coach Kieran McKenna at Portman Road.

The left-back has appeared 51 times for the Red Devils since his debut in 2019.

Williams, who made five appearances for United across pre-season this summer, spent the 2021/22 season on loan with Ipswich's rivals Norwich City.

The youngster played for United's Under-21s during the majority of the 2022/23 campaign and captained the side on many occasions in Premier League 2.

Williams has revealed he is looking forward to linking up with his former U18 coach McKenna in the Championship.

He told the United website: "Kieran was my youth team coach at United and, from there, we have had a great relationship.

"He has always had the standards of a coach at the top level and has had a big influence on me.

"He has always kept in touch with me to make sure I’m OK and I trust everything he says because he is great to learn off.

"I’m excited to work with him again."
