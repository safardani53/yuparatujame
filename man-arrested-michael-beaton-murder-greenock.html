Man charged over death of dad who was 'run over and hacked to death with machete'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 4, 2024

A MAN has been charged in connection with the death of a dad who was allegedly run over and killed with a machete.

Michael Beaton, 35, was found seriously injured after allegedly being hit with a car and stabbed on Drumfrochar Road, Greenock, last Sunday.

Michael, also known as Mick, was raced to hospital in Glasgow but died shortly after admission.

Cops probing the death have charged a 25-year-old in relation to the alleged incident.
