Cheating on Kylie Jenner With Transgender Porn Star Mia Isabella: Report

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Last week, we learned that Kylie Jenner suspects Tyga of cheating and may have even hired a private detective in hopes of catching the 25-year-old rapper in the act.

Now, Radar Online is reporting that Tyga’s been caught sending sexually explicit texts to transgender porn star Mia Isabella, with whom he has allegedly carried on a three-year affair.

The website claims to have obtained screenshots that show what appears to be a photo of Tyga’s penis (the torso tattoos match up) that he sent to Isabella.

If the allegations are true, then the affair would have also overlapped with Tyga’s relationship with Blac Chyna.

An attorney for Tyga claims the whole thing is an elaborate setup orchestrated by a rival artist.

Tyga has not spoken publicly about the allegations, but the fact that the alleged affair was uncovered shortly after Kylie hired a detective at her family’s insistence does not bode well for the rapper.

Tyga and Kylie had reportedly been planning to go public as a couple after her 18th birthday next month.

The news of Tyga’s alleged infidelity comes the same week that Kylie’s half-sister Kourtney Kardashian dumped Scott Disick upon learning that he cheated on her while on vacation in France.
