McDonalds stabbing horror as several people injured and teenage boy arrested The Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 1, 2024

IT'S feared 'several people' are injured as a teen has been arrested at a McDonald's in Manchester this afternoon following reports of a stabbing.

Police were called to the fast food restaurant in the Harpurhey Shopping Centre at 2.35pm.

A teenage boy has been arrested on suspicion of GBH and has been taken into custody for questioning.

The North West Ambulance Service told the Manchester Evening News a male was taken to hospital after being stabbed in the leg.

His age is currently not known.

The statement added: "Two rapid response vehicles and an ambulance attended the scene in Harpurhey at around 3.40pm."

Police have said it is not linked to terrorism.

Pictures on social media show a heavy police presence and paramedics outside to restaurant.

One witness said a "college kid" was stabbed following an altercation and their alleged assailant ran into the McDonald's.

They added the alleged attacker had a "massive knife".
