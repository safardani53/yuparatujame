Home Bargains shoppers going wild for mum and daughter Barbie set which is perfect for twinning with

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, June 17, 2024

LIVING like a Barbie girl in a Barbie world has become all-the-rage as the iconic toy doll makes a highly anticipated appearance on the big screen this month. 

Brands have jumped at the chance to give shoppers a taste of the Barbie life, including Home Bargains. 

Shoppers have unearthed rails and shelves at the discount store with pink merch, clothes and dolls spilling off them. 

That includes matching Barbie hoodies for mums and daughters who love the doll.

The baby pink hoodie comes in adult and kids sizes, with a glittery Barbie logo emblazoned on the front.

However, the hoodies aren’t in every store, so it’s pot luck whether you can nab a £8.99 kids hoodie for your tot and a £12.99 adult one for yourself.

Gemma David, a mum from Leeds, found the matching hoodies in her local store and shared it on the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK Facebook group.

There was also £2.49 flip flops, £1.99 phone cases, £3.99 make-up bags, £1.99 shower caps and £1.99 glitzy key rings. 

Among Home Bargains’ nationwide Barbie offering is the Hair Dressing Kit With Hairdryer for £6.99.

Bosses dubbed it “perfect for little girls who want to style their hair just like Barbie's”.

The set comes with a safe and no heat up battery operated hair dryer, eight clips and two bobbles, a hairbrush and mirror.

There’s even a Barbie electric toothbrush being flogged for just £3.99.

“Maintaining good oral hygiene may not sound too fun, but that's because you haven't got Barbie to help,” a blurb on the product reads. 

“This fabulous Barbie battery-powered toothbrush is sure to get any little girl excited about brushing her teeth. 

“With a slim handle, which is easy to hold, and extra-soft (but effective) bristles, you may have to wrestle it off her.”

For fashion-focused Barbie fans, there’s a clothing tie-dye kit with a doll for £18.99.

Little girls can tie-dye two dresses for their beloved doll to wear, meaning her outfits will be one-of-a-kind.

What’s more, the kit can be used on human clothes with the eight tie-dye powders included.
