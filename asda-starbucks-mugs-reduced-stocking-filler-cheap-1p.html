Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 29, 2024

BARGAIN hunters are sprinting to their nearest Asda stores, desperate to check out the new Starbucks mugs that have hit the shelves.

So if you’re a Starbucks fan, know someone who is, or are struggling with ideas for stocking fillers, you’ve come to the right place.

Not only are the stunning mugs great for a warm hot chocolate and marshmallows on a wintery evening, but they’re sure to leave many impressed if they opened one this Christmas.

Savvy shoppers have taken to social media to show off the cheap mugs, which were put on shelves for only £1 - yes, you heard that correctly.

Asda shopper Donna Blossom Hill shared a post on Facebook, on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, a private group with a staggering 2.3 million members, to show off the bargain buy mugs.

Alongside images of the mugs, as well as some cheap instant coffee sachets that are also available in Starbucks’ iconic Christmas flavour - toffee nut latte - she said: “Asda Toffee Nut Latte (box [of] six) scanned at £1.50 not £2.

Another savvy shopper, Sophie Lauren, took to the Facebook group to show off her purchase, which she was lucky enough to nab for just 1p.

She wrote: “Just been to Asda (Preston), these cups are scanning at £0.01 on scan and go.”
