Matriarch A Horror Movie Is Set To Be Released On Hulu Soon

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Matriarch, Hulu’s next horror film, is set to premiere on the site on Friday, October 21, 2022, at 12 a.m. ET (tentative). The plot centers on a lady who works in the advertising sector and chooses to take a break from her hectic career to see her estranged mother. However, while there, she finds that things aren’t as quiet as they seem. Jemima Rooper plays the lead in the film. Sarah Paul, Keith David Bartlett, Franc Ashman, and many more round out the cast. Ben Steiner, a well-known filmmaker, wrote and directed Matriarch.

Matriarch on Hulu: Plot, Trailer, and More Details

On September 14, 2022, Hulu published the official trailer for the film, which gives a look inside Laura’s incredibly hectic existence. Laura is shown on the phone informing her mother that she overdosed, after which her mother invites her to her home. The second part of the video shows how Laura’s life turns for the worst once she visits her mother in a remote English village.

Overall, the trailer is unrelenting, with a dismal tone that lovers of psychological thrillers and horror will undoubtedly like. According to Hulu Press, the official summary of the film is as follows:

“After a near-fatal overdose, Laura flees the high-stakes pressures of her advertising industry to return to her origins. Laura accepts her estranged mother’s invitation home, hoping that the time away in the remote English town would help soothe the demons racing inside her.

According to the description,

“She quickly realizes that the town’s residents are all hiding an unspeakably awful secret – a mystery that includes not just her mother but also her own dreadful fate.”

Viewers should expect a disturbing thriller that goes deep into the darkest parts of the human psyche based on the teaser and description. Fans of Goodnight Mommy, Conjuring, and classics like The Exorcist and The Omen should definitely see this one.

More information about the Matriarch cast

Laura is played by actress Jemima Rooper in the film. In the film’s trailer, Rooper is in excellent form, portraying her character’s anxiety, terror, and tension with breathtaking ease. Her powerful performance sets the tone for the rest of the film. Rooper has already been featured in a number of notable films and programs, such As If, The Black Dahlia, and Lost in Austen, to mention a few.

Kate Dickie, who seems sinister in the teaser, co-stars with Rooper in another pivotal role as Laura’s mother. The gifted Scottish star is expected to provide outstanding performance. Dickie has been featured in television programs such as Tinsel Town, The Witch, and Prometheus. Fans of Game of Thrones will identify her as Lysa Arryn. Matriarch, the latest horror film, will be available on Hulu on Friday, October 21, 2022.
