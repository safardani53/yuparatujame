Shocking moment girl gang brawl with shopkeepers at wig store in south London

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 23, 2024

THIS is the shocking moment a girl gang brawls with shopkeepers at a wig and beauty shop in south London.

The scuffle is said to have started as a shoplifter tried to make off with items from the Woolwich store.

Punches are hurled by the women as they are held back by men thought to work at Sabina Hair and Cosmetics.

One man simply holds one of the group back at arms length before pushing her to the ground.

She continues to swing at him, yelling "are you dumb, are you dumb".

Another woman is filmed being led away by two men, before another attacks one of them.

She is put into a headlock and forced to the floor.

One of the group tries to run down an aisle away from the chaos, but is chased by a man.

The footage was posted on Twitter by a stunned witness on Saturday, who added: "The owners actually did their best and didn't really hit the girls.

They just protected themselves. I don't know how they did that."

A person commenting on the video said: "I used to go to this shop and go when I'm in London, and the owners are actually lovely.

"They're known in our community. So those girls are out of order for real."

Others criticised the shopkeepers for being forceful with the group, but many defended their actions.

One person one woman said: "My parents had a shop and the struggle to survive is hard. Long hours and you don't really take much home after overheads etc.

"These kids think they can just walk in and take what they want and fight back like they been done wrong."

The Sun Online has attempted to contact the shop for comment.

The Metropolitan Police were unable to confirm any details of the brawl.
