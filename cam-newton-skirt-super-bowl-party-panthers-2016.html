Skirt At Super Bowl Party? The Panthers QBs Big Dilemma Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 30, 2024

The premier QB made headlines earlier this week after he was seen wearing a pair of golden skin tight Versace silk pants. He’s considering outdoing that outfiy by bringing a skirt with him to wear after possibly winning the biggest game of his life. “He can’t decide on which of his off-the-runway concoctions to bring,” a source close to Cam reveals EXCLUSIVELY to HollywoodLife.com. “He has a closet with more Versace regalia inside it than Victoria Beckham, making it hard for him to make up his mind. He bought this beautiful black leather skirt after seeing Jaden [Smith] wearing a similar one and thought he’d bring that but so far, he hasn’t committed.”

As HollywoodLife.com previously reported, Cam is currently doing hours upon hours of interviews leading up to the game on Feb. 7. But instead of being interviewed by a seasoned football journalist, Cam was interviewed on by non-other than Snoop Dogg, 44.

“Is the kid in you outweighing the man in you in this big game?,” Snoop asked. Cam, who chuckled when he realized Snoop was asking him the question, said: “I just think half of me tries to be as professional as possible, but the human in me just wants to take it all in,” Cam explained. “So it’s hard for me to give you, like, a direct answer without bringing both sides out. Of course, the professional side, you still have to go through coming up with any type of edge, because you’re still preparing for a team, but all the festivities, the media, everything leading up to it all — all you see is Panthers/Bronco convo on TV — it’s hard not to just take it all in. It’s a dream come true and hopefully we are prepared come Sunday.”
