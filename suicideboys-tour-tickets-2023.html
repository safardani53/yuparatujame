$ucideboy$ tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 25, 2024

THE POPULAR hip-hop duo $ucideboy$ will be hitting the road in 2023 for a headliner tour.

Here's what fans need to know about purchasing tickets for the upcoming shows.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

How can I get tickets for the 2023 $ucideboy$ tour?

The group, which also goes by Suicideboys on social media, will be hitting the road in August 2023 for a cross-country tour.

Tickets for the $ucideboy$ tour will go on sale Friday, May 19, 2023.

Presale tickets will also be available for the show starting on May 18, 2023.

Fans can purchase tickets online through Ticketmaster, here, or on the band's website, here.

Who are the $ucideboy$ members?

$ucideboy$ was first founded in 2014 by two cousins in New Orleans, Louisiana.

The duo consists of $crim and Ruby da Cherry.

Over the years, $ucideboy$ has released countless songs and 18 albums, including their most recent release, DIRTIESTNASTIEST$UICIDE, which includes the hit songs Champagne Face and My Swisher Sweet, But My Sig Sauer.
