Family rally around Ryan Seacrest's 'sick' ex-girlfriend Shayna Taylor as she holes up in $2million

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 30, 2024

FAMILY are rallying around Ryan Seacrest's ex-girlfriend Shayna Taylor as she holes up in her $2million home - after feeling"sick" following photographs of her ex spotted with a mystery blonde on the beach in Cabo, The Sun can exclusively reveal.

The Live with Kelly and Ryan host, who announced his split from the chef and influencer after more than seven years this week, previously admitted their on/off relationship was a rollercoaster and already seems to have moved on, while his ex is nursing more than a broken heart.

"She's been a little under the weather so her family are keeping her in considering what's going on right now," a source told The Sun, adding her illness is "not serious."

"Her mom Brenda has mostly been taking care of her. When it comes to the split, she's doing great, but she's not ready to talk about it.

"Her family have just rolled their eyes at the news Ryan appears to be moving on so quickly."

The source added that she doesn't need multi-millionaire Ryan to keep a roof over her head, saying: "She owns her own home, and she's very successful in her own right."

The Sun can confirm the deed for the lavish $2,350,000 new build is listed under the influencer's company, Shayna Holdings, and was bought on April 26, 2019.

Shayna, 28, also spent a lot of time at Ryan's Beverly Hills estate, which he reportedly purchased from Ellen Degeneres in 2012 for more than $30million.

The influencer has been flooded by well wishes from fans on her Instagram account over the past few days as she continues to share content on her page, showing her relaxing at home and preparing food in her kitchen.

One follower wrote: "Shayna, I just heard you and Ryan broke up once again!! my heart hurts for you, you deserve so much more than the years you have put into that relationship, your prince will come and you will know it's RIGHT! God bless you!"

The entrepreneur runs her own health and wellness site, Shayna's Kitchen, along with Bottle & Stone, a collection of CBD-based remedies and skincare products.

Ryan, 45, was pictured in Cabo holding hands with a bikini-clad mystery blonde while relaxing on a sun lounger, while his rep confirmed his recent breakup.

A spokesperson for the TV and radio host told the Daily Mail: "Ryan and Shayna decided to end their romantic relationship amicably some time ago.

"They remain good friends, each other's biggest supporters and will always cherish their time together as a couple."

The Sun can reveal Ryan and his new lady love vacationed at the exclusive El Dorado Golf and Beach Club after jetting in from Los Angeles.

It has attracted stars such as George Clooney and Cindy Crawford over the years and is spread across 520 secluded acres.

A shirtless Ryan was seen poolside as his stunning companion showed off her gym-honed figure in a strapless white thong bikini, bucket hat and shades.

As recently as last month, he gushed about his then-girlfriend Shayna on Live with Kelly and Ryan, but hinted at trouble, telling viewers: "I just want to say, happy third anniversary to Shayna."

He explained: "It is our third time together... so we've gotten together, broken up, gotten together, broken up."

Co-host Kelly teased Ryan saying that it wasn't really an anniversary, to which he replied: "No, it's an eight-year run, but it's our third run together. So we celebrate each trial.

"You know, when we drive by an amusement park, I always look at the roller coaster and go, 'Look, there we are'. It's all definitely upside down loops."

Just last month, Ryan - who is often said to be one of the "busiest people in Hollywood" - revealed he was under a lot of stress.

He denied rumors that he had suffered a stroke last month after concerned fans noticed him slurring his speech during the American Idol finale.

The Sun has reached out to Shayna for comment.
