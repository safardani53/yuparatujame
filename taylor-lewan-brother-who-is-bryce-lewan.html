Taylor Lewan Brother: Who Is Bryce Lewan?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 2, 2024

Taylor Lewan, the former Tennessee Titans offensive tackle and free agent, has a younger brother, Bryce Lewan, who grew up playing together in Sacramento.

While Taylor made his name in the world of football, his younger brother also marked his younger brother Bryce Lewan, his name in the realm of hockey. 

Let’s explore more about Taylor Lewan’s family – including his brothers, parents, and their influence on his journey.

Taylor Lewan is an American football player who primarily plays as an offensive tackle in the National Football League (NFL).

He attended the University of Michigan Wolverines and gained recognition as a college football offensive lineman from 2010 to 2013.

In the 2014 NFL Draft, Taylor Lewan was selected in the first round as the 11th overall pick. 

He quickly established himself as one of the league’s top offensive tackles and was known for his athleticism and aggressive playing style.

Lewan’s multiple Pro Bowl selections solidified his status as a top NFL player in his career.

Dominating as an offensive lineman, he became one of the NFL’s highest-paid in 2018 with a lucrative contract extension from the Titans.

Throughout his career, he achieved 2 Big Ten Offensive Lineman of the Year, 3 Pro Bowls, and 2 First-Team All-American titles.

Taylor Lewan retired on February 10, 2023, following a 2022 knee injury, with the Titans officially releasing him twelve days later.

Taylor Lewan Brother: Who Is Bryce Lewan?

Bryce Lewan is the younger brother of the American football offensive tackle Taylor Lewan. 

While Bryce Lewan was born on June 21, 1994, his elder brother was born on July 22, 1991. This means that the brothers have an age gap of 3 years.

The Lewan brothers, from Sacramento, began their sporting journey together in childhood but eventually pursued different career paths.

While Taylor made a name for himself in the NFL as an offensive lineman, Bryce pursued a career in hockey.

The retired hockey player showcased his ability and contributed to his teams both offensively and defensively.

His journey took him through various leagues, including AAA, NAHL, MetJHL, and ACHA III. 

Furthermore, Bryce is a graduate of Pioneer High School and Western Michigan University.

Moving towards his personal life, Lewan has been happily engaged to Chuva Syl since June 20, 2022. 

Scrolling the former hockey player’s Facebook and Instagram pages, we learned he currently works as an artist. His social media platforms are full of pictures that showcase his painting skills.
