Who is Freddie Flintoffs wife Rachael Wools and do they have children?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 2, 2024

IT'S safe to say that Andrew 'Freddie' Flintoff is one of the most recognisable faces on TV.

Although he keeps a lot of his family life private, he is married to former model Rachael Woods.

Who is Rachael Wools Flintoff?

Rachael Wools Flintoff, 42, is a former model and entrepreneur.

From the age of 19, she ran a successful events company called Strawberry Promotions.

She met Freddie Flintoff in 2002 at the Edgbaston cricket ground in Birmingham while at an event to promote her business.

Rachael was formerly signed to the Storm Models agency and her pictures have appeared in various magazines.

How many children do Rachael Wools and Freddie Flintoff have?

Rachael and Freddie have four children - Holly, 17, Corey, 16, Rocky, 13 and Preston, who is two.

The couple's fourth child may have come as a welcomed surprise after the Top Gear host previously admitted that he didn't think he and Rachael would have any more children after Rocky.

In 2016, he told The Sun:"We thought about it. I originally wanted five but you have three and that felt like enough. I couldn't go back to the nappy stage now."

On December 14, 2022, Freddie's son Corey broke his silence after his 45-year-old dad was airlifted to hospital after a horror crash.

The Top Gear star's son said his dad is "lucky to be alive" following the terrifying high-speed smash.

The accident happened whilst he was shooting an episode of Top Gear at Dunsfold Park Aerodrome.

After the incident occurred, on-site medics rushed to Freddie's aid.
