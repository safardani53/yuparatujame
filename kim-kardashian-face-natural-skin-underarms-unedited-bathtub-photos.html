Kim Kardashian shows off REAL face &amp; natural skin including 'pores &amp; rippled underarms' in unedited

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

KIM Kardashian has shown off her real face and natural skin, including her pores and rippled underarms in unedited bathtub photos.  

Kim, 41, took a dip in the tub to promote her most recent SKNN product.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians alum ditched her makeup routine and revealed her true face while getting soaked in water.

Not only was the Kardashians star bare-faced, her blonde hair was also uncombed and drenched.

The Hulu star stretched out her wet arms and revealed her rippled underarms.

She pointed the SKINN Oil Drops to the visible pores on her face.
