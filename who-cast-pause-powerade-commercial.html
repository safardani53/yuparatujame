Who is in the cast of the Pause is Power Powerade commercial?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 19, 2024

FIRST launched in 1988, Powerade is a brand of sports drink created by The Coca-Cola Company.

In March of 2022, Powerade released a commercial featuring a slew of famous athletes.

Who is in the cast of the Pause is Power Powerade commercial?

Simone Biles

Born on March 14, 1997, Simone Biles is an Olympic medal-winning gymnast and multi-World Champion.

Biles won multiple gold medals at the 2016 Rio Olympics, followed by a brief performance in the 2020 Games.

She partially withdrew from the competition to focus on her mental health and safety.

The 25-year-old powerhouse is tied for first place as the most decorated gymnast of all time.

Antonio Conte

Born on July 31, 1969, Antonio Conte is an Italian former professional football (soccer) player and current manager and head coach of the Premier League club, Tottenham Hotspur.

During his time playing with Juventus, Conte became one of the club's most decorated athletes.

He began working on the managerial side in 2006 and has gone on to work with a multitude of teams including Chelsea, Siena, and Bari.

Between 1994 and 2000, Conte played on the Italy national team.

Tom Daley

Born on May 21, 1994, Tom Daley is an Olympic diver and television personality.

During the 2020 Olympics, Daley earned a gold medal in the men's synchronized 10-meter platform event.

Diving since the age of seven, Daley is a member of the Plymouth Diving Club in England.

He worked on ITV's diving reality series Splash! in 2013 before the show was canceled the following year.

Chris Webber

Born on March 1, 1973, Chris Webber is a former NBA player and sportscaster.

Webber played as number four for both the Sacramento Kings and Golden State Warriors.

Prior to his professional career, Webber was a basketball player at the University of Michigan.

Webber has since joined the cannabis industry, launching a private equity fund to support minority-owned marijuana businesses.

Jason Williams

Born on November 18, 1976, Jason Williams is another former professional basketball player.

Throughout his career, Williams was a point guard for the Sacramento Kings, Memphis Grizzlies, Miami Heat, and Orlando Magic.
