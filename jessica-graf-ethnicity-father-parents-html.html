American reality TV personality, Jessica Graf would not have imagined she would work on a show that she used to watch with her father during her young age.

Jessica Graf Engaged To Boyfriend Cody Nickson

The Big Brother star met her partner in Big Brother itself as she started a showmance with contestant Cody Nickson. Other contestants led by Paul Abrahamian often bullied the duo.

But they became a team and made it through the rough time in the show. And the couple didn’t just end their showmance but took it to real life and have been together since.

The pair were given the combined name, Jody by fans, and they started their self-proclaimed YouTube channel, Living Jody. They upload vlogs and show glimpses of their life together in the content.

Jessica Graf’s Bio: Age, Nationality, Ethnicity

Born on 11 December 1990, Jessica Furtado Graf was raised in Cranston, Rhode Island later moving to Los Angeles, California.

Jessica, aged 27, shares an American nationality with a mixed ethnicity of half Italian and half Brazilian; her mother, being Brazilian and her father being an Italian descent.

She lost her father in 2015 and didn’t hesitate to share her admiration for him on her blog. Jessica and her father used to watch Big Brother every summer, little did he know, his daughter would be on the show.

Jessica's Role In Big Brother

The 27-year-old model appeared on the 19th season of Big Brother in June 2017. The reality game show follows a group of contestants who live together in a custom-built home under constant surveillance with complete isolation from the outside world.

Jessica had many feuds with other contestants throughout her stint. She most notably got caught up in a dispute with Paul Abrahamian and eventually became a fan favorite.

Big Brother was the major platform that gained Jessica an intact fanbase and helped her move forward with her career and find her destined one.
