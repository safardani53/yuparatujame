Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

THE Wendy's valley crispy chicken sandwich, which fans believe has been axed, is secretly available if you know what to say.

Fans of the food chain have shared their hack for still being able to purchase their discontinued favorite.

It was reportedly axed to make room for other chicken sandwich variations such as the grilled chicken ranch wrap.

YouTuber Chris Eats Stuff posted a video of him putting the secret menu to the test by trying to get a Wendy's valley chicken sandwich sandwich.

"It's a secret menu item that I've heard about. I've been told they used to have it [and] that they should have all the ingredients for it still," he told viewers.

The burger consists of a crispy chicken patty in a bun with bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayo.

As Chris approached the drive-thru he explained to the server that he wanted the old burger that he heard was still secretly available.

Without any hesitation, the server confirmed that it was possible to get that for him.

"Voila here it is the special menu valley crispy chicken sandwich," Chris said following his successful trip.

"It's really good. Wow, they should have this on the regular menu," he said.

"It's surprisingly better than I thought it was going to be...I'm going to give this one an 8.6 out of 10.

"Very good I would recommend checking it out."

According to some Wendy's experts, the way to get the burger is to explain what it consists of without using the name.

Those who are ordering online are advised to order a crispy chicken BLT and remove the American cheese.

In the comments, one viewer said: "Looks pretty good! 8 bucks, though..."

Chris replied saying: "Ya it’s kinda expensive, but it’s so yummy, it’s almost worth it lol"

However, others added "You can customize with Wendy's app with unlimited regular toppings" so can make your own valley crispy chicken sandwich without needing the secret hack.
