Home Alone fans horrified as movie's iconic pizza order jumps from $122.50 to a whopping $287 by 202

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

HOME Alone fans have been left stunned after learning how much the film's iconic pizza order would cost today.

In the holiday classic, Kevin McCallister's (Macaulay Culkin) brother Buzz (Devin Ratray) is tasked with ordering enough pizza for the family, which cost quite a bit at the time but would now truly break the bank.

The McCallister family were big spenders in Home Alone, with dad Peter (John Heard) and mom Kate (Catherine O'Hara) shelling out for a trip to Paris, lavish gifts and some truly decadent meals.

At one point in the film, Kevin's big brother Buzz orders the family $122.50 worth of pizza from Little Nero's, according to Esquire.

The film was released in 1990 and set around the same time.

Since then, the United States has seen the prices of goods, including food, rise significantly.

Today, that same order would run the McCallister family a whopping $288.

BIG SPENDERS

That's not the only place the McCallister family threw down the big bucks.

Their American Airlines flight for 15 from Chicago to Paris on Christmas day, which included 11 coach tickets and four first-class ones, would cost about $35,320.

Esquire also estimated that it would have cost Kevin's father about $1,200 to fix the basement stairs Kevin destroyed protecting himself during the film.
