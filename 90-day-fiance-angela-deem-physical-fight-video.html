90 Day Fiance's Angela Deem slaps and punches woman as hotel staff holds star back in bloody brawl c

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 3, 2024

90 DAY Fiancé's Angela Deem has gotten into a physical fight in a New York City hotel with a female friend in shocking footage exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun.

The incident went down in the early hours of August 2, after the two had just attended the Reality Lip Sync with other reality stars.

The 90 Day Fiancé star and Jennifer Dilandro of Dolce Aesthetics - the brunette shown in the videos - got into an argument inside their cab prior to arriving at their hotel, an insider told The U.S. Sun.

Upon arriving at their hotel just before 2 am - the Hilton Garden Inn Midtown Park Avenue - the two pals' argument turned physical.

In one video, the two are going at it in the hotel lobby and a receptionist is seen trying to break them apart.

Jennifer is heard repeatedly screaming: "Get the f**k off of me!"

Then, a receptionist shouts: "Please stop!"

In another video, Angela, 57, is seen leaning on top of Jennifer, who is knelt down in front of a couch.

Jennifer is again screaming: "Get off of me! Get off of me!" as Angela appears to be pulling her brunette locks.

An eyewitness told The U.S. Sun that the duo were fighting in the hotel and staff tried breaking them up.

They went their separate ways because, at that point, NYPD arrived on the scene and questioned both ladies.

Eventually, they started yelling at each other again, per the eyewitness.

In a third video, Angela is seen walking on the NYC streets barefoot and telling cops: "I didn't do sh*t."

ANGELA EXPLODES

In a fourth video, Angela is seen with her luggage attempting to get up to her hotel room.

The reality star drops F bombs at her friend: "F***ing mob, Housewives b**ch... You'll always [wanna] be a star.

"You're just like Tonya. You'll never gonna be a star b**ch, you'll always wanna be a wannabe, b**ch.

"I loved you but b**ch, you just wanna be confronted with this one. So seeing my f***ing a**.

"Best friend or not. I don't need her to touch me."

She then acts asks a couple, who are in shock watching the fight go down, in the lobby: "Can you help me into my room?"

The epic fight got so physical, The U.S. Sun has learned Angela started bleeding on her arm because she has thin skin and she bleeds easily.

The blood had gotten on Jennifer as well and, because of blood on the scene, the ambulance was called.

However, no one was taken to the hospital.

A Public Information Officer for the NYPD told The U.S. Sun: "Yes we got a 911 call at 1:31 am at the address you provided. There were two females possibly fighting. They were friends.

"Both individuals refused to give information to police. No one was arrested. Both refused medical attention."

When reached for comment, a Hilton Garden Inn supervisor told The U.S. Sun: "I am not aware of any situation."

A rep for Angela did not immediately respond to The U.S. Sun's request for comment.

Jennifer exclusively told The U.S. Sun after publication: "Angela and myself hold a close bond. Due to unfortunate events, things got heated.

"No one hit each other - it was more pushes and shoves.

"It looks worse on video than what it was."

She continued: "The blood came from my jewelry scratching her and it made her bleed because she's on medication that makes her bleed easily.

"We have since discussed and cleared our friendship."

FIGHTING FOR A CAUSE

Prior to the physical fight going down, the ladies attended Reality Lip Sync Battle at Slate NYC.

The proceeds from the event sales were donated to Give Kids The World Village, an organization that provides cost-free vacations to kids with critical illnesses.

Dolce was one of the event sponsors, along with Kyle Cooke's sparkling cocktail drink, Loverboy.

Kyle, Jill Zarin, Chris Kirkpatrick and Linda Larkin were the judges.

The battle was between stars from Jersey Shore, Big Brother, Survivor, Love Island, Love Is Blind and The Amazing Race.

LOVE JOURNEY

TLC fans were first introduced to Angela when her and her husband at the time, Michael Ilesanmi of Nigeria, starred on season two of 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days.

They also were part of season three where fans saw them back together.

Following that, they appeared on 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? season seven which aired in August 2022.

The couple broke up after fans learned about Michael cheating on Angela on the show.

The on-again, off-again couple was last reported broken up in January 2023.

However, in March there were reports that they were back together after reconciling.
