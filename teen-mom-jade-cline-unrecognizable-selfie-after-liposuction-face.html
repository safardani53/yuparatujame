Teen Mom Jade Cline looks unrecognizable in a new selfie after getting liposuction on her face

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 14, 2024

TEEN Mom Jade Cline looked unrecognizable in a new selfie after she got liposuction on her face.

The reality star posted the photo to her Instagram Stories, appearing confident, months after receiving liposuction to her neck.

Jade documented her recovery on Teen Mom 2, as she also underwent a Brazilian butt lift.

Earlier this month, the cosmetologist showed off her post-surgery figure on social media, striking a pose near a palm tree in California.

In the first snapshot, the Teen Mom star wore a striking red and pinkish outfit with a matching miniskirt.

In the second snap, Jade showed off more of her long and bare legs. 

The TV star captioned the photos: "Cali sun hits different."  

Back in June, Jade showed off the "six-month" results of her cosmetic procedures wearing a tight bodysuit that was a combination of blue, white, and orange colors. 

Jade also curled up the long strands of her blond hair. 

The MTV star captioned the short vid: "All you guys are asking for full body pics."

Jade also gave a shoutout to Doctor Oliver Chang in the caption.

Jade's "scary" procedure

The 24-year-old opened up about her scary Brazilian butt lift procedure during the Teen Mom 2 reunion on Tuesday. 

During the episode, Jade sat down with co-star Briana DeJesus, 27, to thank her for nursing her back to health after she went under the knife for the plastic surgery.

Both MTV stars admitted they thought Jade was going to “die” before Briana stepped in to help.

Recalling the experience, Briana said: “It was bad. What I walked into, I didn’t think I was gonna walk into that.

“I really thought she was gonna die because the way that she was positioned, she wasn’t getting oxygen into her lungs.”

The TV personalities both stated that Jade was “blue,” while the mother of one praised Briana for helping give her a shower for the first time after surgery.

Jade said it “looked like I was bleeding to death” in the shower, while she added it was “amazing” of her friend to help her in the recovery process.

While holding back tears, Jade said: “You’re like a little angel that came and saved me because, for real, I don’t know what would have happened if she wouldn’t have come. It was so painful.”

The mom-of-one added: “I think I would’ve died.”

Emotional reunion

The emotional portion of the reunion continued as Jade broke down in tears.

While speaking about Briana and her friend Shirley Burgos, Jade said: “I think she was there to pick me up when I was really down and I felt like her and Shirley did so much stuff to help me and be there for me.

“Laying in that bed for so long, I was praying to God, like, ‘God, please help me through this.’

“I felt like her and Shirley were like my answer."

Fans got to watch Jade’s recovery process during the most recent season of Teen Mom 2.

During a previous episode the show, Jade dealt with long hours of suffering after her Brazilian butt surgery.

Jade was wheeled out of the hospital while she wore gray sweatpants and had on a shirt that hid the compression bodysuit, which was required after the Brazilian butt lift.

She also had a bandage around her head, where the doctors had extracted fat from her neck.
