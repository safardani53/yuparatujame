Who is Molly Yeh's husband?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024

FOOD enthusiast, Molly Yeh, and her husband announced they are pregnant with their second child in September 2021.

The couple announced the birth of baby No. 2 on Wednesday, February 23, 2022.

Who is Molly Yeh's husband?

Molly Yeh, 32, met Nick Hagen, 35, while they were both attending Juilliard School of the Performing Arts in New York City.

Hagen is a musician and was working to master the trombone while Yeh was studying percussion.

They moved in together in Brooklyn and in 2013, the two decided to move from the city to Hagen's farm in the East Grand Forks, Minnesota.

They got married one year later and live on the sugar beet farm that has been in Hagen's family for generations and now supplies ingredients for most of Yeh's recipes.

The couple welcomed their daughter Bernie, whose full name is Bernadette Rosemary Yeh Hage, in 2019.

The family loves to go hiking together in their spare time, according to Yeh's social media accounts.

When did Molly Yeh and Nick Hagen have their second baby due?

People exclusively revealed in September 2021 that Molly Yeh and Nick Hagen are expecting their second child.

"We're so excited to add a new little bean to the family!" Yeh told People. "Bernie is already training to be a big sister with her Cabbage Patch doll, and she's doing great!

"She keeps suggesting that we name the new baby Bernie, so we're still working on names."

Who is Molly Yeh and what is her net worth?

Television personality and ultimate foodie, Molly Yeh, was born in Glenview, Illinois to Jewish and Chinese parents.

During her time in New York, she fell in love with food and even started her own blog.

She graduated with a B.A. in 2011 from Juilliard School and is estimated to have a net worth of $8million.

What is her Food Network show about?

Yeh's show, Girl Meets Farm, made its debut on the Food Network in 2018 and has run for nine seasons.
