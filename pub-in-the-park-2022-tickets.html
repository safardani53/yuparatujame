Pub in the Park 2022: Tickets, prices and locations

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 4, 2024

CELEB chef Tom Kerridge and his foodie mates are going on nine stop tour and it's going to be bigger than ever.

With nine destinations, incredible food and performances from huge acts - you don't want to miss out.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

If you're a food fan then you won't want to miss this.

With tickets available to buy now from See Tickets, you'll need to be fast as they're sure to sell out soon.

Tickets are priced from £43.40, but this will depend on which venue and time you book.

Child Tickets are priced at £27.13 , and VIP options are also available.

Revellers can expect performances from the likes of Craig David TS5, Rag’n’Bone Man, Kaiser Chiefs, Professor Green, Sister Sledge, Supergrass, General Levy, and many more.

There'll also be appearances from The Feeling, Judge Jules, McFly across the various venues - which include Marlow, Wimbledon, Warwick, Bath, Dulwich, Tunbridge Wells, Chiswick, St Albans, and Brighton.

Of course, you can't forget the food, with none other than Tom Kerridge, Michael Roux Jr, Hairy Bikers, Simon Rimmer, James Martin, Grace Dent, Andi Oliver, Matt Tebbutt and more hosting across the sites, with a number of gorgeous food stalls, artisan food and drink markets, mixologists and chef demos as well.

Line up's and appearances will change depending on your chosen venue - so make sure you check before you buy.

The brain child of Tom Kerridge, the chef recently posted about the event on Instagram, saying " PUB IN THE PARK TICKETS ARE ON SALE NOW! Our 2022 tour is bigger and better than ever! The rush is on for tickets, with a stellar music line-up, incredible food, and the nation's best chefs - it's set to be our biggest party yet. Can’t wait ❤️"

Want to get tickets? You'll need to be fast, but you can get yours from See Tickets here.

Where is Pub in the Park?

Pub in the Park takes place in Marlow, Wimbledon, Warwick, Bath, Dulwich, Tunbridge Wells, Chiswick, St Albans, and Brighton.

How much are Pub in the Park tickets?

Tickets are priced from £43.40 onwards, but will depend on when and where you buy your tickets.

Child and VIP ticket's are also available.

Pub in the Park opening times

Pub in the Park opening times will vary where you book, there are afternoon and evening sessions, this will depend when you book.
