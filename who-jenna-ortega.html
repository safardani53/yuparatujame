Who is Jenna Ortega? | The Sun

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, September 28, 2024

JENNA Ortega has been trending after she was caught smoking with a pal.

Here's everything you need to know about cigarette-wielding actress Jenna Ortega.

Who is Jenna Ortega?

Born September 27, 2002, Jenna Ortega is known as an American actress.

She first stepped into the entertainment industry back in the early 2010s after appearing in films like Iron Man 3 and Insidious: Chapter 2.

Since then, she has gone on to star in several productions, including Jane the Virgin, You, Stuck in the Middle, The Babysitter: Killer Queen, The Fall Out, Scream, and Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous.

She has also lent her voice to the popular Disney series, Elena of Avalor.

In May 2023 Jenna Ortega was caught smoking while out and about with a friend.

The star and fellow actress Gideon Aldon were spotted in Notting Hill, West London.

The two American actresses enjoyed a day out while enjoying drinks and having a smoke — despite a no-smoking sign above them.

The pair were also spotted having a conversation while sitting at a table while taking a few selfies.

Jenna wore a see-through long-sleeved shirt with a blank tank top underneath as well as green cargo pants and boots.

The Wednesday star wore her hair in a ponytail with her bangs flowing in the wind and a pair of sunglasses on top of her head.

A video of Jenna smoking went viral in an online forum to share their thoughts on the actress taking part in the bad habit.

Jenna's mom Natalie, a nurse, slammed her daughter's smoking habit in an Instagram Story.

She posted a series of photos on her Instagram story that explain the health risks of smoking.

Natalie listed lung cancer, cigarette stench, damaged teeth and gums, cataracts, brittle bones, and oral cancer.

Before she went into the details, she posted a meme of Gollum from Lord of the Rings.

The meme said: "Don't believe those lies about cigarettes being bad for you. I've been smoking for most my 21 years and I feel great!"

She also posted a screenshot that said: "The problem with smoking is that most people find the smell to be repulsive. That quickly counters anything about the visuals that look 'cool.'"

Another post read: "No matter who you are and how big you get, always surround yourself with the people who will tell you the truth."

What is Jenna Ortega's net worth?

Due to her successful career, Ortega has been able to amass an extensive net worth.

As of June 2022, Ortega's estimated fortune is $3million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This number mostly comes from contract negotiations.

In 2022, Ortega will debut as the title character in Netflix's new series, Wednesday.

She has also reprised her role as Tara Carpenter in Scream 6 and appear in the upcoming film, Finestkind.

What films and TV shows has Jenna Ortega been in?

Most recently, Jenna has taken on the role of Wednesday Addams in Netflix series Wednesday.

Other acting credits include: You, The Fallout, The Babysitter: Killer Queen and American Carnage.

Since starting her acting career, Ortega has been nominated for several awards.

In 2018, she was nominated for the Best Lead Actress in a Feature at the Southampton International Film Festival, and then the following year she was nominated for the Best Young Actor - Television award at the Imagen Foundations Awards.

While she may have lost those, she ultimately won the 2018 Imagen Award for Best Young Actor - Television and the 2022 MTV Movie + TV Award for Most Frightened Performance.

What age is Jenna Ortega?

Jenna Ortega is 20 years old.

She was born in Coachella Valley, California, and is the fourth of six children.

The actress began her career as a child star, playing the title character in Jane the Virgin.

Ortega nearly became a soccer player, and is a Barcelona fan.

What height is Jenna Ortega?

Jenna Ortega is 5'1", or 1.55m.

The actress revealed that her diminutive stature allows her to run in high heels.

Does Jenna Ortega have a boyfriend?

It's thought that Jenna Ortega is currently single.

The star tends to keep her personal life private which includes her love life.

She regularly updates her followers on her Instagram page, but her posts tend to be about her current acting roles.

Back in October 2018, Jenna was romantically linked to Asher Angel after they attended the official Just Jared’s Annual Halloween Party and dressed up as Ariana Grande and Pete Davidson.

After that they went on to attend multiple red carpet events together, such as the premiere for Marvel’s Venom.

However, the two never publicly confirmed or denied dating rumors.

Before this, dating rumors with Jacob Sartorius were sparked after Jenna appeared in his music video for his song Chapstick.

But it seems that they never became more than friends.

In February 2019, Jenna cleared up any romance rumours on a podcast episode of Just Between Us.
