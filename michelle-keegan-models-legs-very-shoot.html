Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 4, 2024

MICHELLE Keegan gave her fans a thrill today as she showcased her endless legs in sheer tights and a black leotard.

It was just one of the sexy looks the ex Corrie star tried out in her sultry photoshoot for online fashion brand Very.

Michelle, 33, made the most of her perfect pins in transparent black tights and she even ditched her skirt, opting to show off her hips in the revealing outfit instead.

She teamed the rock star look with a black leather jacket and boots and scraped her hair into a spiky bun.

Other photos show Michelle perched on a white stool as she models a black winter coat and a mini dress.

The brand released the fashionable shots for their Autumn/Winter range after Michelle gave her fans a sneak peak of her collection on Instagram.

She sent her 4.7million fans wild as she posed for a series of sizzling shots in her bedroom - and looked utterly amazing.

Showcasing several looks from her new collection, she first donned a white and black high-neck coord, which accentuated her slim figure.

Michelle then donned a sleek sleeveless berry-coloured bodysuit with a pair of cropped faux leather trousers, before slipping into a comfy monochrome-coloured tracksuit.
