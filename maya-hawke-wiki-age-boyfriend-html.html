Maya Hawke Wiki: Boyfriend, Parents, and Family Detailed

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Maya Hawke is known for her screen debut as Jo March in Little Women. She is also famous as Robin in Netflix's series, Stranger Things. 

Other than that, the actress is also recognized as the daughter of famous parents.

Here's a rundown on her bio with information on her age, net worth, and relationship.

Maya Hawke's Age, Height

Hawke was born on July 8, 1998, in New York City to celebrity parents Ethan Hawke and Uma Thurman.

She is at the age of 23 as of this time of writing. Her height is 5’8,” and her brother is Levon Hawke.

From an early age, Maya has been in front of the limelight, given her father and mother are two of the top actors of their time.

Despite her parent's stardom, Hawke was never forced or carted along every red opportunity. 

In an interview with The Guardian in 2021, Hawke clarified her parents tried to protect her from falling into acting. As a result, they wouldn't include her in even bit-parts in their movies. 

"They wanted to ensure I had a strong enough backbone, my own passion for it, and work ethic," Hawke shared. She also added that they were supportive of her acting choice. 

Maya Hawke's Net Worth

Hawke's budding career as an actress has obviously enriched her net worth.

But it is still far less than her celebrity parents. Her parents, Ethan and Uma, are award-winning actors who boast an incredible amount of net worth.

Ethan has a net worth of $55 million, while Uma holds a net worth of $45 million, as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Maya Hawke's Boyfriend and Dating History

Hawke has had quite an eventful love life. In mid-2018, she was secretly dating a guy named Gus Wenner. 

While it is not known they broke up, news of Hawke's new relationship with fellow actor Tom Sturridge surfaced in mid-2020.

The pair had started dating in August 2020 and flaunted their relationship publicly. They were even noticed enjoying a low-key stroll in New York in December 2021. 

However, their relationship of almost half and a year withered, and the pair split.

Hawke's current boyfriend, Spencer Barnett. (Photo: Instagram)

Months after the breakup, Hawke started dating singer Spencer Barnett, the son of Shaklee Corporation CEO Roger Barnett and former Manhattan district attorney Sloan Barnett. 

The actress and her boyfriend were spotted packing on the PDA in Manhattan on 2022 Valentine's Day.

Hawke cut a casual figure in a blue-and-gold brocade jacket with a matching cape, black cargo pants, and patent leather boots. Her boyfriend wore a blue jacket, grey turtleneck, brown slacks, and darker brown boots.
