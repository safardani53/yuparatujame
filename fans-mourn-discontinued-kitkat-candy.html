Fans mourn discontinued KitKat candy bar they will never see again - and they say it was 'the best o

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 25, 2024

THE Hershey Company is known for its extraordinary variety of sweet treats, but KitKat fans have not forgotten one of their beloved flavors.

The original KitKats were first introduced to chocolate lovers back in 1935 when they were known in London as â€œRowntree's Chocolate Crisp."

There have been dozens of flavors of KitKats since the Hershey Company took over KitKat production in the U.S.

Favorites include milk chocolate, dark chocolate, white creme, mint and dark chocolate "Duos", birthday cake, lemon, and apple pie.

While flavors come and go often, there is one special KitKat flavor that a certain candy-lover just couldn't seem to get over.

The fan reached out to the candy company on a Facebook post promoting the KitKat chunky, an extra thick version of the yummy bar.

Read more on Hershey Company

"What happened to the cookies and cream KitKat?" the woman asked.

The Cookies and Cream version featured crushed cocoa cookies kneaded into white chocolate.

Sweet cream was layered between the crisp wafers to bring together the unique flavor.

"They were the best ones and can't find them," she added.

Sadly, the Hershey company did not have great news for the candy-lover.

"Hi Jennifer, unfortunately, we discontinued the KitKat Cookies & Cream, so they are no longer available. Have you tried our new KitKat Chunky Salted Caramel Popcorn?"

Another customer added to the conversation, questioning why the company would pull one of their favorite flavors.

"Why was it discontinued please?" another customer wrote. "It's the best one."

The company responded, offering some hope to the Cookies and Cream KitKat fans.

"We'll let our team know that you want to see this back, Odichinma!" they wrote.

Rare Cookies and Cream KitKats made in Japan can still be found on Amazon.
