Jeremy Meeks Modeling Photos: Released! Ravishing!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 16, 2024

Jeremy Meeks has gone from mug shots to head shots.

Nearly two years to the day after going viral due to his unparalleled good looks, Meeks is back in the news due to… well… his unparalleled good looks.

Back in June of 2014, Meeks was arrested on weapons charges.

But most Internet users looked right past his crimes and honed in instead on his piercing blue eyes, earning Meeks the nickname of Hot Mug Shot Guy.

The World Wide Web can be an amazing place, can’t it?

It all started with the following booking photo, which has amassed 101,000 Likes, 12,757 shares and 26,377 comments.

We’re guessing it’s the most popular photo in the history of the Stockton Police Department Facebook page.

A short while later, Meeks signed a professional modeling contract.

Sadly, however, he could not get this phase of his life started immediately because he was sentenced to two years in prison. How unfair, right?!?

Now, however, three months after his release from prison and a little over one year since he signed with White Cross Management, Meeks has shared his first two professional modeling pictures on Instagram.

And it’s clear the guy still has it.

“#warriors #dubnation #hometeam,” Meeks wrote as a caption to the first photo featured above, paying homage to the Golden State Warriors as they battle for the 2016 NBA title.

“Count down #housearrest #bayarea,” he wrote about this image directly above, hinting perhaps at a larger spread to come.

Speaking of which… we bet lots of women around the world would spread their legs for Jeremy Meeks, if you know what we mean!

This guy may have spent time behind bars for gun possession, but he clearly has a bright future ahead of him because many members of the opposite sex would like to possess his gun in their hands, mouth or private area.

We’re talking, of course, about Meeks’ penis in this metaphor.

Ogle away, ladies!
