9 Best Dog Christmas Outfits 2021 | The Sun UK

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 3, 2024

GETTING festive for the Christmas season is something humans enjoy a lot, from decorating our homes to getting our sparkle and tartan on.

Of course, anyone with a four-legged friend also knows that the best dog Christmas outfit is an obligatory investment for man's best friend.

Now, for anyone with a baby, small child, or furry four-legged friend, Christmas is all about the outfits: Christmas pudding bobble hats, coordinated family Christmas pyjamas, dog elf costumes…

Dressing your dog up for Christmas can take many different forms.

They can become your own personal reindeer in a dog reindeer costume or offer a twist on the typical Santa Claus look in a dog Santa outfit.

Of course, you also kit your dog out in a festive Christmas jumper or coat – something to keep them cosy on brisk Boxing Day walks.

Nothing is more on-trend – or creates a better photo op – than twinning with your dog and wearing matching Christmas costumes or jumpers. Joy to the world indeed.
