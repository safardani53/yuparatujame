What You Didn't See During The Season Finale Of Survivor: Millennials Vs. Gen X

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024
During the season finale, David crafted together a fake Immunity Necklace that Jay eventually discovered and used at Tribal Council. (Sadly, Jeff Probst told him it was a fake and the real-estate agent was sent packing.)

When the moment played out on TV, the entire cast gave David a rousing and well-deserved round of applause, which caused the TV writer to light up. All's fair in love and Survivor, right?

In case you missed it, check out the two-hour season finale of Survivor and the one-hour Live Reunion show on CBS All Access.

Plus, watch every full episode of Survivor: Millennials Vs. Gen X, as well as every season of the series, on CBS All Access.
