Becky G and Sebastian Lletget Show Their Love on Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, April 20, 2024

Becky G is not hiding her devotion to her beau, Argentinean soccer star Sebastian Lletget. The "Mayores" singer welcomed the new year with her boyfriend and her family at a cozy cabin, where the couple shared this romantic kiss.

On New Year's Day, she shared this sweet photo of her loved ones:"Although I woke up extremely sick, there is no other place and no other group of people I’d rather be with to bring in the new year. This is my core. I love them with my whole heart," she captioned the pic.

On her love's birthday, Sept. 3, she celebrated him in a sweet post: "Happy birthday to my person! I'm happy you were born. I'm beyond grateful to be a part of your journey in life. I can't thank god enough for bringing our paths together."

Her birthday wishes continued with another caption and frame-worthy snap: "I'm so sad that I can't be with you on your special day but I promise we will celebrate when we both get back home! I love you! Hope you have a special day. I wish you many more years of health, positivity, strength, success, and most importantly, happiness."

She shared this tender embrace between the two on June 25, writing: "I got your back. You got mine. It's pretty simple."

She posted this image of the two flaunting their sexy bods on a bright summer day, June 3: "Just me and my person," her caption reads. "Through the highs and lows he manages to hold it down for me. I'm grateful. I like doing life with you. You're real."

The singer posted these cute pics on Mar. 6 and wrote: "Missing my better half."

The petite brunette gave a shout out to the athlete on Oct. 16 of 2016 with this adorable shot and message: "Have a great game today baby!"

Lletget was her date to the Grammy Awards on Feb. 12 last last year:. "After Grammy business with my amor," she wrote.

While watching her soccer stud's game on TV on Oct. 26 of 2016, Becky donned his sports jersey and cheered him on, after lighting candles to her Guardian Angel and the Virgin of Guadalupe.

The singer, here with Lletget vacationing in California on July 3 of 2016, told E! News: “When you have someone that is your best friend, your family, your lover, your homie and all in one, it’s like, ‘Wow.’ You don’t have to think about it, it doesn’t take much effort, you know what I mean? It’s just that support and it feels really beautiful.”

Becky shared this sweet snap of her and her prince at Disneyland on July 1 of 2016: "Happiest place on earth with the one that makes me the happiest," to which Lletget replied: "My love, you make me so happy."

On June 16 of 2016 the duo exchanged a tender kiss. "Real one. I'm a lucky woman," she captioned the pic.

The pair arrived hand in hand at the American Music Awards in 2016. "Thank you to my man for standing by my side," she captioned the pic.

Becky G was swept off her feet, literally: "To that one time in Vegas at 3:30 a.m. deciding we'd take our first Christmas picture together. Santa brought him early to me this year. Best present ever," she wrote.
