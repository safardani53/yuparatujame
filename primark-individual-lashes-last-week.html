Primark fans go wild over its 1 individual lashes - and they last a week

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 7, 2024

FORGET expensive falsies, as Primark fans are scrambling to get their hands on £1 individual lashes that last a week.

With its affordable prices, Primark has long been a favourite for many bargain-hunting fashionistas and beauty lovers.

Full of dupes for high-end cult favourites, such as its copy for the iconic Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk range, the store is definitely not one to sleep on.

Amongst the fans of the bargain store is the social media user @crazycatladyoftiktok who regularly shares all things hair and beauty on TikTok.

This time, the make-up enthusiast took to the platform to share a bargain discovery - a set of individual lashes that cost her just £1.

In the now-viral video that's been viewed close to a million times, the stunner claimed that not only are these affordable, but that you will have wispy lashes for up to a week.

But instead of using the glue that comes with the lash extensions, which she had purchased in Medium, the make-up lover grabbed a tube of Invisi-Lash Glue by Tatti Lashes.

After applying some of the product underneath her natural lashes, the TikTok user placed each of the fake lashes underneath the lash line with tweezers.

''This is such a quick and easy method and if you do look after them, they will last for up to a week,'' she claimed.

Once she had placed all of the hairs on, she squeezed them with her fingers to bond them together.
