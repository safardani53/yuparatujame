I'm a money expert - here's how the codes on Sam's Club price tags will help you spot deals

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

SAM'S Club is renowned for offering brand-name products at affordable prices.

And it turns out if you know what to look for on price tags, you can find the best deals in the store.

TikToker and financial expert Vincent Chan, Hivincentc on TikTok, explained the secret language Sam's Club uses on its labels.

In a video, Vincent points out that the price, as well as other letters on the tag, can clue you in on deals.

If you spot a price tag ending in one cent, that product is on sale.

But looking more closely at the bottom left corner of Sam's Club price tags can tell you a lot more.

Sam's Club is said to use letter codes to indicate whether an item is a staple product, on sale, or coming off shelves soon.

If you spot a C above the barcode on a price tag, that means the product is on clearance, Vincent said.

The price might not be marked down if the item was just put on clearance, but that item may be off shelves soon.

Code S means the product is seasonal, and will only remain stocked for a limited time.

Items with an O on the tag are "One Time Buys," and are available for a limited time only.

S and O labels, like C, don't necessarily mean the item is at its lowest price, but these products will be marked down if they don't sell out.

Lastly, Sam's Club marks staples with A or N.

A - meaning active - indicates the item will be stocked indefinitely, but you probably aren't getting a deal.

N, for never out, means you can expect to see the product at Sam's Club forever.

The Sun contacted Sam's Club for comment.
