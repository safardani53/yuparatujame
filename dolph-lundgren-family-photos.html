See Photos of the Actor With His Kids Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Dolph Lundgren is a Swedish actor, who’s starred in ‘Rocky IV’ and ‘Creed II.’ Check out photos of him and his kids here.

Dolph Lundgren has been an action movie staple since making his Hollywood debut in 1985 with ‘Rocky IV.’ After becoming the beloved villain Ivan Drago, Dolph has appeared in a number of films and TV series, including playing He-Man in the ‘Masters of the Universe’ movie. After spending much time directing his own flicks, Dolph returned to blockbuster action movies with fellow veterans like Arnold Schwarzenegger and Sylvester Stallone for ‘The Expendables’ movies. He even reprised his role as Ivan Drago in the critically acclaimed ‘Creed II’ movie.

Throughout his life, Dolph was married twice, both to women who have mostly stayed out of the spotlight. His second marriage to Anette Qviberg lasted from 1994 until 2011, and the pair had two daughters Ida and Greta, who have shown interests in acting and photography, respectively.
