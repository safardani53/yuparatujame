Heidi Klum films herself totally naked in the shower as she gets ready for AGT leaving fans speechle

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

HEIDI Klum has stripped totally naked in the shower while getting ready for America's Got Talent.

The supermodel, 50, filmed herself washing her hair in a marble bathroom ahead of the live finale on Tuesday.

Heidi posted a clip of herself naked with her back to the camera as she stood under the showerhead.

The AGT judge turned slightly to the side, before filming her legs as the water ran down them.

Heidi then got out of the shower, wrapped herself in a white bathrobe, and put on a gold under-eye mask on.

The German catwalk star sipped a cup of coffee and ate some eggs, before putting on a sheer black crop top and high-waisted pants.
