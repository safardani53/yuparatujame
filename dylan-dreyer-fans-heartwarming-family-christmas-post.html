Todays Dylan Dreyers fans are all saying the same thing after host shares heartwarming family

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 1, 2024

FANS are all saying the same thing after Dylan Dreyer shared a sweet family Christmas post with a video of her son.

The TV meteorologist for NBC News and host on Today's 3rd Hour shared an adorable video over the weekend of herself walking through her home while an assortment of loud banging noises is heard in the background.

The video then concludes with Dylan revealing what was causing the noise.

Her eldest son, dressed in red Christmas pajamas, was seen playing on his new drum set on a landing area halfway up a staircase.

When she finally walks up to her son, she's heard saying: "Nice job, buddy!"

Her son was full of excitement as he donned a huge smile and lifted his drumsticks in the air.

Dylan captioned the post, which received hundreds of comments from fans: "Merry Christmas indeed!!!"

Most of the comments were warning Dylan of the endless noise she'd be hearing.

Some described her as brave for purchasing him a drum set.

One fan wrote: "Because if you play drums in the stairwell they can hear you on all the floors. Nice strategy!"

Another commented: "Clearly a gift from an aunt, uncle or grandparents.

"No sane parent would buy their son a drum set!!"

"Merry Christmas Dylan and family. You are very brave with that drum set," wrote a third.

A fourth person said: "Parents of the decade if you actually allowed Santa or did this to yourselves.

"That's a present a bratty uncle gives to torture Sis or Bro."

The third-hour meteorologist, who first appeared on Today on September 15, 2012, also shared a cute selfie with her husband, Brian Fichera, and their three sons, Russell James, Oliver George Fichera, and Calvin Bradley Fichera.

Earlier this month, Dylan's fans spotted an upsetting detail in the meteorologist's family photo, also shared on Instagram.

The Today star shared a snap with her husband and their three sons at a bowling alley.

Dylan had baby Russell, one, in a sling around her neck and carried Oliver, two, on her hip.

Brian stood next to her and their eldest son Calvin, five, posed at the front of the group.

The family was out celebrating Calvin's birthday, which was on December 16.

Dylan captioned it: "Pure chaotic joy at Cal’s birthday party. Let birthday week begin!"

Some fans took issue with the fact that Dylan was holding two of her young sons, while her husband stood next to her without helping.

"Why are you carrying two kiddos?" one asked, and another added: "Sorry, But WHY CAN'T BRIAN CARRY THE KIDS??!! DANG ...DYLAN YOUR BACK IS WORTH SAVING."

A third posted: "Ok Brian, why are you letting Dylan carry Oliver…slacking again."

The backlash comes after Dylan revealed Russell and Oliver were rushed to the hospital with a terrifying respiratory virus.

The meteorologist, 41, said that she felt "helpless" as they battled Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) in October.

Both of her sons have now recovered, with Oliver back at school and "happy as can be."
