ABC News Correspondent Marries Longtime Partner Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

The wedding took place at the Walton House, which is a historic English-style cottage in Florida, according to a report by The New York Times. The event was officiated by Tommy’s sister, Megs A. DiDario, who is a Universal Life minister.

Gio and Tommy met on Instagram in January 2015, and have enjoyed a whirlwind romance ever since. It’s clear these two could not be happier with each other — looking at their adorable wedding pics.

The pair got engaged back in September, 2015 in the most romantic of proposals. Gio got down on one knee in front of the Eiffel Tower, during their European vacation together, and popped the question. “‘Time is what’s left behind in the wake of love,'” Gio wrote as a caption for the pic showing the romantic gesture on his Instagram.

Gio is best known for his work on World News Tonight With David Muir, as well as 20/20 and Nightline. He’s also appeared on Good Morning America.

It looks it was just the perfect day for these two lovebirds, good luck to them because they seem so perfect for one another and clearly were meant to be!
