Why did Kailyn Lowry delete the photo of her posing naked and pregnant with a horse?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 19, 2024

TEEN Mom's Kailyn Lowry uploaded a nude Instagram photo of herself pregnant and posing with a horse while she was on vacation earlier this year.

She swiftly deleted the photo after an hour of it being live on the platform.

Kailyn did not immediately reveal why she took the photo down, but later said she "trusted" a photographer's vision.

What is the photo of Kailyn Lowry posing with a horse?

Back in March, Kailyn uploaded a picture to Instagram of her posing naked and pregnant with a horse while she was on vacation in Hvolsvöllur, Iceland.

The photo was her first nude pregnancy photo that she uploaded while she was pregnant with newborn Creed.

She posed in nothing but a pair of hiking boots as she stood by the horse.

Why did she delete it?

The picture was only up on Instagram for an hour before she deleted it.

"I already screenshot the last pic 😂😂," one fan wrote on her next upload.

"Why did u delete ur naked pic?? Who cares what ppl think," one fan asked.

"Why'd you delete your weird naked maternity picture?" another said.

At the time, Kailyn did not reveal why she uploaded and then deleted the photo, but on a recent episode of her podcast Coffee Convos she said that she trusted a photographer's creative vision.

Kailyn said: “I never want a photographer to give me this idea and tell me it’s amazing and have me post it and I’m trusting this photographer. 

“I never want to do that again.”

Does Kailyn have an OnlyFans?

Only a few months on from her nude Instagram post-and-delete, Kailyn revealed on her podcast that she had started an OnlyFans account.

She said she plans to upload naked pictures to the paid subscription content service, and is charging fans $20 a month for access.

She told co-host Lindsie Chrisley: “I made an OnlyFans! It’s like a private Instagram that people pay for.

“You make money on it… You post nudes and send feet pics.”

After Lindsie asked if she planned on uploading lewd videos and photos, Kailyn explained that that’s how people make most of their money on the platform, with someone she knows making $100 “for her feet pics.”

Who is Kailyn Lowry?

Kailyn Lowry shot to fame on MTV's 16 and Pregnant and Teen Mom.

  • Kailyn Lowry is from Pennslyvania.
  • She has four children, all boys, from a newborn to 10 years old.
  • Two of her children have separate fathers, while her last two share the same father.

The Teen Mom added that she’s “for sure gonna send feet pics,” as well as nude photos.

Before Kailyn posts any sexual snapshots, though, she plans to have her co-host approve each picture in order to not have “another horse pic situation happen ever again.”

How many kids does Kailyn have?

Kailyn is mom to Isaac, 10, Lincoln, 6, Lux, 2 and newborn Creed.

The reality TV star gave birth to her fourth son on July 30.

Kailyn recently shared that her fourth child likely won’t be her last.

She reflected on her pregnancies on her podcast and shared that when she was pregnant at 18 she never “would have pictured myself” having four kids.

Lindsie joked that Kailyn seems “like somebody who would have six,” with Kailyn adding: “Six is my max.”

How many baby daddies does she have?

The teen mom’s first child came from a relationship with boyfriend Jonathan Jo Rivera.

She gave birth in January of 2010 to her son, Isaac Elliott Rivera.

Kailyn married the father of her second child, Javi Marroquin, a member of the US Air Force, in September of 2012 while pregnant.

In 2013, she gave birth to Lincoln Marshall Marroquin.

Her marriage was documented on Teen Mom, as Javi blamed Kailyn for suffering a miscarriage.

The two began having marriage issues and appeared on Marriage Boot Camp, but ultimately divorced in 2015.
