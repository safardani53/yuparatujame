Zain Retherford (Wrestler) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

Zain Allen Retherford is an American freestyle wrestler and graduated folkstyle wrestler who competes at 65 kilograms. In freestyle, Retherford is a two-time US World Team Member, in 2019, placing 27th at the World Championships, and in 2017, placing 21st at the World Championships. Additionally, he helped Team USA reach third-place at the 2019 World Cup, going undefeated at 4–0, was the '19 US Open National runner-up and was the '12 Cadet World Champion at 63kg. One of the greatest Penn State Nittany Lions of all time, Retherford placed fifth at the 2014 NCAA Division I National Championships, and afterwards redshirted '14–'15. Subsequently, Retherford went 93–0 through his next three seasons, winning three NCAA National championships, three Big Ten Conference titles and multiple high-importance awards, such as the Dan Hodge Trophy, Big Ten Wrestler of the Year and NCAA Most Dominant Wrestler, between others.
