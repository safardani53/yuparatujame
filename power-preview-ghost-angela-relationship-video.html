Ghost &amp; Angelas Relationship Turns Serious &amp; Theyre Not Alone Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 20, 2024


“Jamie you think it went alright with the kids?” Angela (Lela Loren) asks Ghost (Omari Hardwick) in a clip for this week’s Power, exclusive to HollywoodLife.com. “I think it went great. We’ll work it out. We’ll make it work and we’ll be a family.”

Of course he says the right thing — but their conversation stops there, and they’re not exactly as alone as they think they are. “We’ll have everything we want,” he assures her. Naturally, she can’t help but bring up what happened.

“Speaking of getting everything you want, things alright at the club? You left me alone with the kids the first time they came over. It must have been important,” she asks him. However, when he immediately starts apologizing, she lets him know she’s not upset — just worried.

Here’s the official episode synopsis:

Ghost is presented with a business opportunity that could take the clubs to the next level, but must outshine devious new rivals, all while looking over his shoulder for Lobos’s revenge. Tommy reunites with Tasha in a business scheme, while he also tries to keep a new drug contact under control and protect his reputation in the streets. Tasha and Ghost are tested when Tariq crosses a line.

The new episode of Power, “I Got This on Lock,” airs Sunday, July 31 at 9/8c on Starz.
