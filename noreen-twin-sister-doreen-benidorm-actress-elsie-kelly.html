Who plays Noreen's twin sister Doreen in Benidorm and who is actress Elsie Kelly?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024

VIEWERS thought they were seeing double when Noreen Maltby's twin sister Doreen first arrived in Benidorm.

But who plays Doreen? And what will her storyline involve? Here's what we know...

Who plays Noreen's twin sister Doreen in Benidorm?

Noreen's twin sister Doreen will arrived at the Solana Hotel in episode 6 on April 11.

Both characters are actually played by Elsie Kelly - who has played the part of Noreen since series one in 2007.

Viewers quickly learned that the sisters are like chalk and cheese, but that doesn't stop the hotel staff and guests from getting the pair mixed up.

Glamorous Doreen makes her debut when she orders a sherry from barman Mateo (Jake Canuso), who mistakes her for coffee-ordering Doreen.

Quickly recognising her sister's voice, Noreen looks overjoyed as she turns to see her twin and the pair greet one another with a great big hug.

But it looks too much for Mateo to take in as he rubs his eyes and appears to get a little too excited at the sight of twins - even if they are in their eighties.

Doreen has been described as the "more upmarket, posh sister" who has also escaped to Benidorm for sun.

Who is the actress Elsie Kelly?

Elsie Kelly, 81, is an actress from Merseyside.

Aside from her role in Benidorm she has also appeared in various other screen roles, which included playing gossipy cleaner Mrs Tardebigge in Crossroads from 1986 to 1988.

After a long break from TV she starred in the 1996 film Intimate Relations.

She has also appeared in an episode of E4 drama Skins and played Joan the cook in the 1996 adaptation of The Famous Five.

More on Benidorm

When did Noreen arrive in Benidorm with her daughter Pauline in series 10?

Benidorm regulars Noreen and daughter Pauline (Selina Griffiths) arrived at the Solana Hotel on episode five.

Alcoholic Pauline was suffering from writers block, as she attempted to write her first novel.

But after falling off the wagon, she started to write an erotic novel, much to mum Noreen's disgust.
