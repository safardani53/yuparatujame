Kenan and Kel Reunite on Jimmy Fallon: Welcome to Good Burger!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, March 30, 2024

If you’re a child of the ’90s, there’s a good chance you spent some time enjoying the talents of Amanda Bynes, Kenan Thompson and Kel Mitchell on the beloved sketch show All That.

Needless to say, the show’s best-known stars have experienced very different fates in the years since they left Nickelodeon.

Thompson went on to star on SNL; Bynes’ struggles with mental illness have been well-documented in the tabloids; and Mitchell seemed to fall off the face of the Earth.

Fortunately, we’re living in the age of nostalgia and Jimmy Fallon.

Yes, if the Tonight Show host was able to reunite the cast of Saved By the Bell, surely he can save any former child star from the depths of obscurity (except for Screech).

So it’s not surprising that Mitchell got a standing ovation when he proved last night that he can still deliver his signature "Good Burger" catchphrase as only Ed the Good Burger Guy can.
