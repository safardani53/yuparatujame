Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

BEYONCE has treated fans to a sexy bum snap while promoting her IVY PARK x adidas collaboration.

The 38-year-old megastar shared the promo snap to her Instagram account on Tuesday.

In the photo, Beyonce seductively looks over her shoulder as a single sneaker dangles from a backpack.

The smouldering booty shot showcases her enviable curves and toned legs in which she models a fitted purple bodysuit and sock stiletto boots from the Ivy Park line.

While in another snap, Bey sets pulses racing with a shot of herself facing away from the camera.

Her perfectly rounded behind can be seen peeping out from her long braids.

The pictures were from an official advertisement which the singer posted to Ivy Park's Instagram account on Tuesday.

The caption read: “adidas x IVY PARK January 18 #adidasxIVYPARK.”

Fans were quick to comment on the shot, with one writing: "If I had that body, I'd walk around like that all day, shoe and all."
