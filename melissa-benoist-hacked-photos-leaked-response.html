Glee Actress Fires Back Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Melissa is just one of two Glee actresses whose alleged naked pics have leaked, as part of the massive celebrity hacking scandal.

Melissa, 25, who plays Marley on Glee has fired back, in response to her breach of privacy.

The star tweeted “incivility should not be supported,” out to her nearly 350,000 Twitter followers, and included a link to a Ted talk, covering the topic of click bait news.

My sentiments exactly, on so many levels, @sallykohn …incivility should not be supported. Please watch. https://t.co/XgMu2TRPbE

Although Melissa’s alleged revealing phots were not leaked with the initial photos, which included A-listers Jennifer Lawrence, Ariana Grande, Victoria Justice and Kate Upton, the pics hit the Internet shortly after, and we applaud Melissa for standing up for herself!

Melissa’s costar Becca Tobin, 28, who plays Kitty on Glee was also a celeb victim to this horrifying hacking scandal, and she took to Twitter to address the issue, too.

Becca took a slightly more comedic approach, tweeting, “Merry XXXmas!” after her alleged photos showed her decorating a Christmas tree sans clothing.

Matthew Morrison aka Mr. Schuester tweeted back at Becca in support, saying, “Way to embrace it Hun!”

Glee superstar Lea Michele is also on the list of alleged celebs who have revealing images floating around. We hope that Lea’s private pics are not blasted out on the Internet!
