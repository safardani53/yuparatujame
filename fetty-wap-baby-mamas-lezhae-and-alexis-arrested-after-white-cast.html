Lezhae Lowder and Alexis Skyy: Fetty Wap Baby Mamas Get Into Knife Fight at White Castle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 17, 2024

It’s been over a week since Blac Chyna fought Alexis Skyy, and she may want to rethink challenging Alexis to Round 2.

Alexis got into a fight with Lezhae Lowder. Both women are Fetty Wap’s baby mamas.

Things escalated when Lezhae allegedly pulled a knife and, as you’ll see in this video, things did not go her way.

Alexis Skyy gave birth to Alaiya Grace just over a year ago.

Alaiya, Fetty Wap’s daughter, was both premature and had to spend 100 days in the NICU.

Lezhae Lowder has two children with the rapper and Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood star.

Her daughter Zaviera was born in March of 2015; and her son, Aydin, was born in March of 2018.

Yes, you did the math correctly — Aydin and Alaiya were born just two months apart.

Clearly, the two mothers struggle with getting along.

TMZ reports that Lezhae was arrested after she pulled a knife on Alexis.

But that’s not how things started.

Apparently, Alexis and her entourage were partying at a New Jersey bar … one where Lezhae just happens to work.

Lezhae allegedly tried to fight Alexis during that, which is … a choice.

She reportedly threw a bottle at Alexis’ car as the other woman was leaving.

Unfortunately, it did not end there.

According to the initial report (and yes, there is a new, conflicting report), Lezhae tracked down Alexis.

Alexis and her pals had stopped by a White Castle (yes, really) in Patterson, New Jersey.

This, TMZ reports, is where Lezhae allegedly caught up to Alexis and wanted to finish her would-be fight from earlier.

When Lezhae rolled up at White Castle and (reportedly) began throwing more bottles, Alexis’ friends exited their own vehicle.

This is when Lezhae allegedly brandished a knife.

Though Lezhae appeared to receive a bit of a beat-down in the ensuing fight, Alexis is said to have stayed out of it.

The newer, conflicting report from sources close to Lezhae tells a different version of events.

Lezhae’s side says that she didn’t follow Alexis there, but that both baby mamas just happened to have the same taste in late night meals.

(To be fair, they do appear to have similar taste in men)

Lezhae says that she feared for her safety when Alexis’ companions jumped out of their car, which is why she brandished the knife.

In the ensuing fight, she says, she was hit by multiple men but insists that she didn’t threaten or harm anyone with the knife.

TMZ has confirmed that Lezhae’s arrest was for disorderly conduct, not for the knife or the fight.

Apparently, she continued to yell at Alexis’ faction even after the arrival of law enforcement officials.

If police aren’t sure of who the guilty party is, they either arrest everyone or the person who appears to be the bigger a-hole in the scenario.

When you’re yelling your head off at people in front of police, they may assume that you are the instigator.

This is a bit of a mess, right?

These women are both adults and, more significantly, they’re both moms.

In fact, their young children are all siblings.

Whatever went down — whether you believe Alexis, Lezhae, or neither — should not have happened.

We’re just relieved that nobody died.
