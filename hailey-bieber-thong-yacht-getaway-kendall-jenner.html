Hailey Bieber rocks just a thong bikini as she poses on luxury yacht getaway with BFF Kendall Jenner

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 26, 2024

HAILEY Bieber has shown off her body in a thong bikini while on her recent getaway to bring in the new year.

The model recently shared moments on Instagram from her vacation alongside her BFF Kendall Jenner.

She posed around the boat as she showed off her slender physique - leaving little to the imagination as she flaunted her behind.

Hailey matched her vacation bikini with a burlap bucket hat, sunglasses, and a gold belly chain that rested along her chiseled abs.
